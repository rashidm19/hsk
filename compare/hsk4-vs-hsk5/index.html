HSK 4 vs HSK 5 / HSK 4 与 HSK 5 的区别

What is expected after HSK 4 — vocabulary growth, advanced grammar, exam format jump

HSK 4 vs HSK 5 完整对比 — 涵盖词汇量、语法点、题型变化、考试时长、能力要求、复习时长全维度对比。基于 HSK 官方考试大纲（2010 旧版与 2025 新版）整理。

New Grammar Introduced / 新增语法点

  • 把字句 advanced: 把+O+一+V (一字句)
  • 被动句 advanced: 被/叫/让+O+给+V+其他
  • 比较句 advanced: A + adj + B + 数量补语 (e.g., 他比我大三岁)
  • 复句 (5 new types): 一旦…就, 假如…就, 万一…就, 哪怕…也, 不但没…反而
  • 让步关系: 哪怕 / 即便 / 即使 (more nuanced)
  • 虚词: 之 / 以 / 而 (literary function words)
  • 成语 (idioms): ~150 four-character idioms introduced at HSK 5
  • 修辞: 比喻、拟人、夸张 (figures of speech)
  • 复杂量词搭配: 一系列, 一连串, 一组 (集合量词)

Study Time Estimate / 复习时长建议

6-12 months for most students who passed HSK 4. The vocabulary jump (1000→2500) and the introduction of essay writing make HSK 5 the biggest single difficulty leap in the HSK series. Plan 2 hours per day minimum.
