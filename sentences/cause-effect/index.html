🔗 Cause & Effect — 因果关系

因为/由于/所以/因此 patterns for explanations. These 10 sentences are battle-tested HSK 4 building blocks — memorize them as whole chunks, then swap in your own vocabulary. Part of the 100 essential HSK 4 sentences collection.

Grammar patterns used in this set / 本组句子用到的语法

因为…所以 (because…so) →由于 / 因此 (due to / therefore) →已经 / 曾经 (already / once) →才 / 就 (timing) →

The 10 sentences / 句子清单

Ordered easiest first. Difficulty: ★ simple · ★★ one clause/connector · ★★★ complex sentence (虽然…但是, 因为…所以…).

1
这件事的结果让所有人都很满意。 ★
Zhè jiàn shì de jiéguǒ ràng suǒyǒu rén dōu hěn mǎnyì.
The outcome of this matter has made everyone very satisfied.
📋 describing result
2
由于天气原因，今天的活动取消了。 ★★
Yóuyú tiānqì yuányīn, jīntiān de huódòng qǔxiāo le.
Due to the weather, today&#x27;s activity has been cancelled.
📋 formal explanation
3
为了准备考试，他每天学习到很晚。 ★★
Wèile zhǔnbèi kǎoshì, tā měi tiān xuéxí dào hěn wǎn.
In order to prepare for the exam, he studies until very late every day.
📋 purpose
4
因此，我们应该更加重视这个问题。 ★★
Yīncǐ, wǒmen yīnggāi gèng jiā zhòngshì zhège wèntí.
Therefore, we should pay more attention to this issue.
📋 drawing conclusion
5
由于他的努力，公司的效率提高了很多。 ★★
Yóuyú tā de nǔlì, gōngsī de xiàolǜ tígāo le hěn duō.
Thanks to his efforts, the company&#x27;s efficiency has improved a lot.
📋 attributing result
6
因为下大雨，所以我们决定不去公园了。 ★★★
Yīnwèi xià dà yǔ, suǒyǐ wǒmen juédìng bú qù gōngyuán le.
Because it was raining heavily, we decided not to go to the park.
📋 explaining decision
7
之所以失败，是因为我们准备得不够充分。 ★★★
Zhī suǒyǐ shībài, shì yīnwèi wǒmen zhǔnbèi de bú gòu chōngfèn.
The reason we failed is that we didn&#x27;t prepare adequately.
📋 advanced explanation
8
既然你已经决定了，我就不再说什么了。 ★★★
Jìrán nǐ yǐjīng juédìng le, wǒ jiù bú zài shuō shénme le.
Since you&#x27;ve already decided, I won&#x27;t say more.
📋 given/since pattern
9
他说话很有道理，所以大家都听他的。 ★★★
Tā shuō huà hěn yǒu dàolǐ, suǒyǐ dàjiā dōu tīng tā de.
What he says makes sense, so everyone listens to him.
📋 explaining behavior
10
我们之所以选择这家公司，是因为它的服务好。 ★★★
Wǒmen zhī suǒyǐ xuǎnzé zhè jiā gōngsī, shì yīnwèi tā de fúwù hǎo.
The reason we chose this company is its good service.
📋 explaining choice
