⚖ Comparison — 比较

比/不如/跟…一样 patterns. Heavy on HSK 4 reading. These 10 sentences are battle-tested HSK 4 building blocks — memorize them as whole chunks, then swap in your own vocabulary. Part of the 100 essential HSK 4 sentences collection.

Grammar patterns used in this set / 本组句子用到的语法

虽然…但是 (although) →比 comparison →不如 (not as good as) →跟…相比 (compared with) →

The 10 sentences / 句子清单

Ordered easiest first. Difficulty: ★ simple · ★★ one clause/connector · ★★★ complex sentence (虽然…但是, 因为…所以…).

1
这个方法比那个方法效果好得多。 ★
Zhège fāngfǎ bǐ nàge fāngfǎ xiàoguǒ hǎo de duō.
This method is much more effective than that one.
📋 method comparison
2
今年的天气没有去年那么冷。 ★
Jīnnián de tiānqì méiyǒu qùnián nàme lěng.
This year&#x27;s weather is not as cold as last year.
📋 negative comparison
3
他的中文跟中国人一样流利。 ★
Tā de Zhōngwén gēn Zhōngguórén yíyàng liúlì.
His Chinese is as fluent as a native speaker&#x27;s.
📋 equality comparison
4
和城市相比，农村的生活更安静。 ★
Hé chéngshì xiāng bǐ, nóngcūn de shēnghuó gèng ānjìng.
Compared with the city, life in the countryside is quieter.
📋 lifestyle comparison
5
这次考试比上次难一些。 ★
Zhè cì kǎoshì bǐ shàng cì nán yìxiē.
This exam is a bit harder than the last one.
📋 test comparison
6
他比我大三岁。 ★
Tā bǐ wǒ dà sān suì.
He is three years older than me.
📋 age comparison
7
我觉得这本书没有那本书有意思。 ★
Wǒ juéde zhè běn shū méiyǒu nà běn shū yǒu yìsi.
I think this book is not as interesting as that one.
📋 preference
8
和他相比，我还有很多需要学习的地方。 ★★
Hé tā xiāng bǐ, wǒ hái yǒu hěn duō xūyào xuéxí de dìfang.
Compared to him, I still have a lot to learn.
📋 humble comparison
9
比起去年，今年的销售额增加了百分之三十。 ★★
Bǐ qǐ qùnián, jīnnián de xiāoshòu&#x27;é zēngjiā le bǎi fēn zhī sānshí.
Compared to last year, sales have increased by 30% this year.
📋 data comparison
10
我的工资不如他高，但是工作比较轻松。 ★★★
Wǒ de gōngzī bùrú tā gāo, dànshì gōngzuò bǐjiào qīngsōng.
My salary is not as high as his, but the work is relatively easy.
📋 balanced comparison
