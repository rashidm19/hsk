❤ Express Feelings — 表达感受

感到/觉得/让 — emotions tested in attitude questions. These 10 sentences are battle-tested HSK 4 building blocks — memorize them as whole chunks, then swap in your own vocabulary. Part of the 100 essential HSK 4 sentences collection.

Grammar patterns used in this set / 本组句子用到的语法

才 / 就 (timing) →

The 10 sentences / 句子清单

1
听到这个消息，我感到非常高兴。
Tīng dào zhège xiāoxi, wǒ gǎndào fēicháng gāoxìng.
I felt very happy to hear this news.
📋 happiness
2
看到他难过的样子，我也很心疼。
Kàn dào tā nánguò de yàngzi, wǒ yě hěn xīnténg.
Seeing him sad, I also feel sorry for him.
📋 sympathy
3
这件事让我很感动。
Zhè jiàn shì ràng wǒ hěn gǎndòng.
This matter really touched me.
📋 moved
4
考试前他非常紧张，几乎睡不着觉。
Kǎoshì qián tā fēicháng jǐnzhāng, jīhū shuì bù zháo jiào.
Before the exam he was very nervous, almost couldn&#x27;t sleep.
📋 nervousness
5
对于这次的成绩，我感到很满意。
Duìyú zhè cì de chéngjì, wǒ gǎndào hěn mǎnyì.
I feel very satisfied with the result this time.
📋 satisfaction
6
我真的很羡慕你能去那么多地方旅游。
Wǒ zhēn de hěn xiànmù nǐ néng qù nàme duō dìfang lǚyóu.
I really envy that you can travel to so many places.
📋 envy
7
失败了不要难过，下次努力就好了。
Shībài le bú yào nánguò, xià cì nǔlì jiù hǎo le.
Don&#x27;t be sad about failure — just try harder next time.
📋 comforting
8
他的成功让大家都感到骄傲。
Tā de chénggōng ràng dàjiā dōu gǎndào jiāo&#x27;ào.
His success makes everyone feel proud.
📋 pride
9
听到他要离开公司，大家都很意外。
Tīng dào tā yào líkāi gōngsī, dàjiā dōu hěn yìwài.
Everyone was very surprised to hear he was leaving the company.
📋 surprise
10
我对自己当时的决定感到后悔。
Wǒ duì zìjǐ dāngshí de juédìng gǎndào hòuhuǐ.
I regret the decision I made at that time.
📋 regret
