📈 Describe Change — 描述变化

随着/越来越/比以前 patterns for time-based change. These 10 sentences are battle-tested HSK 4 building blocks — memorize them as whole chunks, then swap in your own vocabulary. Part of the 100 essential HSK 4 sentences collection.

Grammar patterns used in this set / 本组句子用到的语法

越来越 (more and more) →比 comparison →随着 (along with) →

The 10 sentences / 句子清单

Ordered easiest first. Difficulty: ★ simple · ★★ one clause/connector · ★★★ complex sentence (虽然…但是, 因为…所以…).

1
近几年，中国的发展非常快。 ★
Jìn jǐ nián, Zhōngguó de fāzhǎn fēicháng kuài.
In recent years, China&#x27;s development has been very fast.
📋 recent trend
2
现在的年轻人和以前不太一样了。 ★
Xiànzài de niánqīngrén hé yǐqián bú tài yíyàng le.
Today&#x27;s young people are different from before.
📋 generational change
3
他的汉语水平比以前提高了很多。 ★
Tā de Hànyǔ shuǐpíng bǐ yǐqián tígāo le hěn duō.
His Chinese level has improved a lot compared to before.
📋 improvement
4
这座城市的样子比十年前完全不同了。 ★
Zhè zuò chéngshì de yàngzi bǐ shí nián qián wánquán bùtóng le.
This city looks completely different from ten years ago.
📋 long-term change
5
她的脾气最近变好了很多。 ★
Tā de píqì zuìjìn biàn hǎo le hěn duō.
Her temper has gotten much better recently.
📋 personal change
6
公司的政策最近做了一些调整。 ★
Gōngsī de zhèngcè zuìjìn zuò le yìxiē tiáozhěng.
The company&#x27;s policy has been adjusted recently.
📋 organizational change
7
天气越来越冷，注意多穿点衣服。 ★★
Tiānqì yuè lái yuè lěng, zhùyì duō chuān diǎn yīfu.
The weather is getting colder — make sure to dress warmly.
📋 weather change
8
经过这次失败，他变得更加坚强了。 ★★
Jīngguò zhè cì shībài, tā biàn de gèng jiā jiānqiáng le.
After this failure, he has become even stronger.
📋 experience-driven change
9
随着时间的变化，他变得越来越成熟了。 ★★★
Suízhe shíjiān de biànhuà, tā biàn de yuè lái yuè chéngshú le.
Over time, he has become more and more mature.
📋 gradual change
10
随着科技的发展，人们的生活越来越方便。 ★★★
Suízhe kējì de fāzhǎn, rénmen de shēnghuó yuè lái yuè fāngbiàn.
With the development of technology, life becomes more convenient.
📋 tech impact
