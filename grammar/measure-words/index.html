HSK 4 Measure Words / HSK4 量词专项

8 measure words new at HSK 4 (打/袋/棵/台/幅/场/顿/趟) + 5 borrowed MW (脸/手/盒/屋子/桌子) + HSK 3 review. Each with examples, common-error tips, and an 8-question quiz.

Why Measure Words Matter at HSK 4 / 为什么 HSK4 重视量词

Measure words (量词) are tested in every HSK 4 mock exam — usually in the 选词填空 reading section and 排词成句 writing section. The HSK 4 syllabus formally introduces 8 new measure words plus the special category of "borrowed measure words" (借用量词) where ordinary nouns like 脸/手/盒 act as measure words. Knowing the right MW for each noun is a high-yield, easy-win area.

8 Measure Words New at HSK 4 / HSK 4 新增的 8 个量词

These are formally tested at HSK 4 — none appear in HSK 1-3.

打
dá
noun MW (loanword)

Use: a dozen of (small items, especially eggs/beer)

买一打鸡蛋。
Mǎi yì dá jīdàn.
Buy a dozen eggs.
他喝了半打啤酒。
Tā hē le bàn dá píjiǔ.
He drank half a dozen beers.
💡 Borrowed from English 'dozen'. Same meaning, fixed pinyin (dá, not dǎ which means 'to hit').
袋
dài
noun MW (containers)

Use: a bag of (rice, sugar, snacks, etc.)

妈妈买了一袋大米。
Māma mǎi le yí dài dàmǐ.
Mom bought a bag of rice.
孩子吃了两袋薯片。
Háizi chī le liǎng dài shǔpiàn.
The child ate two bags of chips.
💡 Used for anything sold in a bag/sack. Common in supermarket scenes — frequently appears in HSK 4 listening.
棵
kē
noun MW (plants)

Use: for trees, plants, vegetables with roots

院子里种了几棵苹果树。
Yuànzi lǐ zhòng le jǐ kē píngguǒ shù.
Several apple trees are planted in the yard.
这棵树已经一百多年了。
Zhè kē shù yǐjīng yìbǎi duō nián le.
This tree is more than 100 years old.
💡 Use for things rooted in the ground: trees (棵树), grass (棵草), vegetables (棵白菜). Don't use for cut flowers — those use 朵 (duǒ).
台
tái
noun MW (machines)

Use: for machines, appliances, large devices

我刚买了一台新电脑。
Wǒ gāng mǎi le yì tái xīn diànnǎo.
I just bought a new computer.
这台机器已经用了十年。
Zhè tái jīqì yǐjīng yòng le shí nián.
This machine has been used for ten years.
💡 Use for: 电脑, 电视, 机器, 空调, 冰箱, 洗衣机, 钢琴 (piano). Anything mechanical or electronic that sits on a surface or is treated as a unit.
幅
fú
noun MW (flat art)

Use: for paintings, drawings, calligraphy, maps

墙上挂着一幅美丽的画。
Qiáng shàng guà zhe yì fú měilì de huà.
A beautiful painting hangs on the wall.
他写了一幅书法送给老师。
Tā xiě le yì fú shūfǎ sòng gěi lǎoshī.
He wrote a calligraphy piece for the teacher.
💡 幅 is for FLAT ART that you display: 画 (paintings), 字画 (calligraphy), 地图 (maps), 照片 (photos in formal contexts). For ordinary photos use 张 (zhāng) instead.
场
chǎng
verbal MW (events)

Use: for events that take place: matches, movies, rains

昨天看了一场精彩的足球比赛。
Zuótiān kàn le yì chǎng jīngcǎi de zúqiú bǐsài.
I watched an exciting football match yesterday.
下了一场大雨。
Xià le yì chǎng dà yǔ.
It rained heavily (one rainstorm fell).
💡 Use 场 for events with audience or natural events: 比赛, 电影, 演出, 雨, 雪, 病, 梦. Different from 次 (instance) — 场 emphasizes the EVENT itself.
顿
dùn
verbal MW (meals/scoldings)

Use: for meals, beatings, scoldings, fights

我们吃了一顿丰盛的晚餐。
Wǒmen chī le yí dùn fēngshèng de wǎncān.
We had a sumptuous dinner.
妈妈把孩子骂了一顿。
Māma bǎ háizi mà le yí dùn.
Mom scolded the child (one round).
💡 顿 measures one INSTANCE of eating, scolding, beating, or fighting. 'Three meals a day' = 一天三顿饭. 顿 with 骂/打 always implies the 'session' is complete.
趟
tàng
verbal MW (round trips)

Use: for trips, errands (going and returning)

我去了一趟超市，买了点儿菜。
Wǒ qù le yí tàng chāoshì, mǎi le diǎnr cài.
I made a trip to the supermarket and bought some food.
明天我要去一趟北京。
Míngtiān wǒ yào qù yí tàng Běijīng.
I'm going to Beijing tomorrow (a round trip).
💡 趟 = one round trip (go AND come back). Different from 次 — 次 just counts instances, 趟 emphasizes the going-and-returning action. Common with 去/来.

Borrowed Measure Words / 借用量词

Borrowed MW = ordinary nouns used as measure words. They give vivid, idiomatic descriptions and are tested in HSK 4 reading.

The 4 "Event" Measure Words — Most-Tested Distinction

Many HSK 4 candidates lose marks on 次 / 场 / 顿 / 趟 because all four feel like "time/instance" in English. Quick rule:

  • 次 — generic count of any action (这是我第一次来北京 = my first time in Beijing)
  • 场 — events with audience, weather events, illnesses, dreams (一场比赛, 一场雨, 一场感冒)
  • 顿 — meals, scoldings, beatings (吃了一顿 / 骂了一顿 / 打了一顿)
  • 趟 — round trips, going-and-returning errands (去了一趟超市 / 来了一趟北京)

HSK 4 Measure Word Quiz / 量词测验

Test your understanding with these 8 HSK 4-style fill-in-the-blank questions.

Q1
妈妈刚刚买了一___鸡蛋。
Why: 鸡蛋 (eggs) are counted by the dozen → 打.
Q2
院子里有几___苹果树。
Why: Trees (planted, with roots) → 棵.
Q3
他刚买了一___新电脑。
Why: 电脑 (computer, an electronic device) → 台.
Q4
墙上挂着一___风景画。
Why: 画 (painting, displayed art) → 幅. 张 works for casual photos but 幅 is correct for paintings.
Q5
我们昨天看了一___精彩的比赛。
Why: 比赛 (a match/game, an event) → 场. 次 just counts but doesn't emphasize the event nature.
Q6
我们一家人吃了一___丰盛的晚餐。
Why: Meals are counted with 顿. 一顿饭 is the standard collocation.
Q7
我去了一___北京，办了点儿事。
Why: Round trip (went and came back) → 趟. 趟 emphasizes the going-and-returning.
Q8
他写了一___好字，让人羡慕。
Why: Skill in handwriting → 一手 (borrowed from 手 = hand, treated as MW for skills shown by hand).

HSK 4 真题语境例句 / Real-Exam-Context Examples

Five sentences combining the new HSK 4 measure words (打/袋/趟/台/顿/场/幅/棵) with everyday exam contexts: shopping, family meals, sports events, technology, and homes. Each sentence stacks 2–3 MW so you see the natural collocations together.

我去了一趟超市，买了一袋米和一打鸡蛋。
Wǒ qù le yí tàng chāoshì, mǎi le yí dài mǐ hé yì dá jīdàn.
I made a trip to the supermarket and bought a bag of rice and a dozen eggs.
Test context: 听力 shopping dialogues. 趟 (round trip), 袋 (bagged goods), 打 (a dozen) — three new HSK 4 MW in one natural sentence. Most-tested supermarket frame.
我们一家人坐在一起吃了一顿很好吃的晚饭。
Wǒmen yì jiā rén zuò zài yìqǐ chī le yí dùn hěn hǎochī de wǎnfàn.
Our whole family sat together and had a really delicious dinner.
Test context: 阅读 daily-family passages. 顿 = the canonical MW for meals (一日三顿饭). HSK 4 选词 frequently rejects *一次 here — meal events take 顿, not 次.
昨天晚上看了一场非常精彩的足球比赛。
Zuótiān wǎnshang kàn le yì chǎng fēicháng jīngcǎi de zúqiú bǐsài.
Last night I watched an extremely exciting football match.
Test context: 听力 sports/event dialogues. 场 = MW for events (比赛, 电影, 演出, 雨). HSK 4 stresses 场 over generic 次 — 场 captures the event nature, not just the count.
我刚买的这台电脑比之前那台快得多。
Wǒ gāng mǎi de zhè tái diànnǎo bǐ zhīqián nà tái kuài de duō.
The computer I just bought is much faster than the previous one.
Test context: 听力 product-comparison dialogues. 台 = MW for machines/appliances (电脑, 电视, 空调, 冰箱). Combined with 比…得多 — a multi-pattern HSK 4 test frame.
院子里种了几棵苹果树，墙上还挂着一幅漂亮的画。
Yuànzi lǐ zhòng le jǐ kē píngguǒ shù, qiáng shàng hái guà zhe yì fú piàoliang de huà.
Several apple trees are planted in the yard, and a pretty painting hangs on the wall.
Test context: 阅读 home-description passages. 棵 (rooted plants) + 幅 (paintings/calligraphy) — both HSK 4 new MW. Note: cut flowers use 朵, not 棵; ordinary photos use 张, not 幅.
