HSK 4 Pattern: 一方面…另一方面…

Presents two aspects, reasons, or sides of a situation. Both clauses are equally weighted.

一方面 + aspect A, 另一方面 + aspect B

Examples / 例句

一方面他想去留学，另一方面他又舍不得离开家人。
Yì fāngmiàn tā xiǎng qù liúxué, lìng yì fāngmiàn tā yòu shěbude líkāi jiārén.
On one hand he wants to study abroad; on the other, he can't bear to leave his family.
Two conflicting feelings of equal weight
这份工作一方面工资高，另一方面压力也大。
Zhè fèn gōngzuò yì fāngmiàn gōngzī gāo, lìng yì fāngmiàn yālì yě dà.
This job has a high salary on one hand, but also high pressure on the other.
Balancing pros and cons
一方面要保护环境，另一方面也要发展经济。
Yì fāngmiàn yào bǎohù huánjìng, lìng yì fāngmiàn yě yào fāzhǎn jīngjì.
On one hand we must protect the environment; on the other, develop the economy.
Two equally important goals
锻炼身体一方面能让人更健康，另一方面也能减轻压力。
Duànliàn shēntǐ yì fāngmiàn néng ràng rén gèng jiànkāng, lìng yì fāngmiàn yě néng jiǎnqīng yālì.
Exercising can on one hand make you healthier, and on the other relieve stress.
Two parallel benefits — frequent HSK 4 reading-passage structure
上网一方面给我们的生活带来了方便，另一方面也带来了一些新的问题。
Shàng wǎng yì fāngmiàn gěi wǒmen de shēnghuó dàilái le fāngbiàn, lìng yì fāngmiàn yě dàilái le yìxiē xīn de wèntí.
Going online has on one hand made our lives more convenient, and on the other brought some new problems.
Frames a balanced 利弊 (pros/cons) discussion — very HSK 4 essay-style

Common Errors / 常见错误

✗ 一方面他很聪明，另一方面他考了第一名。
✓ 他不但很聪明，而且考了第一名。
Getting 1st place is a RESULT of being smart, not a parallel aspect. Use 不但…而且 for progressive results.
✗ 一方面下雨了，另一方面我没带伞。
✓ 因为下雨了，而且我没带伞，所以…
These are not two balanced aspects but a cause-effect chain.
Easily confused: 一方面…另一方面 = two equal sides. 不但…而且 = progressive (second is more important). See comparison →

Quick Quiz / 小测验

___工作很有意思，___工资也不低。
Two equal aspects of a job → 一方面…另一方面. (不但…而且 also works but implies the second is more important.)
___要节约用水，___也不能影响正常生活。
Two balanced considerations → 一方面…另一方面.
学中文___能了解中国文化，___对找工作也有帮助。
Two parallel benefits → 一方面…另一方面.

一方面…另一方面… FAQ

When is 一方面…另一方面… tested in HSK 4?

一方面…另一方面… (On one hand... on the other hand...) appears in HSK 4 reading comprehension (Q66-85) when texts use complex sentences, in 阅读排序 (Q56-65) where this pattern's connectors signal sentence order, and in listening 段落 (Q26-45) where the speaker uses it to express presents two aspects, reasons, or sides of a situation.

What's the most common mistake with 一方面…另一方面…?

一方面…另一方面 = two equal sides. 不但…而且 = progressive (second is more important).
