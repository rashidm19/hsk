HSK 4 Pattern: 要是…否则/不然…

Presents a condition and warns about the negative consequence if it's NOT met. 否则/不然 mean 'otherwise'.

要是 + condition, (就)… ; 否则/不然 + bad consequence

Examples / 例句

要是明天不下雨，我们就去爬山。
Yàoshi míngtiān bú xià yǔ, wǒmen jiù qù páshān.
If it doesn't rain tomorrow, we'll go hiking.
要是…就 = simple if-then
你要早点出发，否则会迟到的。
Nǐ yào zǎo diǎn chūfā, fǒuzé huì chídào de.
You should leave early, otherwise you'll be late.
否则 = otherwise (formal warning)
快点写作业，不然来不及了。
Kuài diǎn xiě zuòyè, bùrán lái bù jí le.
Do your homework quickly, or you won't make it.
不然 = otherwise (casual)
你最好按时吃药，否则病可能会更严重。
Nǐ zuìhǎo ànshí chī yào, fǒuzé bìng kěnéng huì gèng yánzhòng.
You'd better take your medicine on time, otherwise your illness may become more serious.
最好 + 否则 + 可能会 = polite warning of a bad outcome — HSK 4 health context
我们得提前预订房间，不然到时候就没地方住了。
Wǒmen děi tíqián yùdìng fángjiān, bùrán dào shíhou jiù méi dìfang zhù le.
We have to book a room in advance, or there'll be no place to stay when we arrive.
不然 + 到时候 = casual contingency warning — HSK 4 travel-planning topic

Common Errors / 常见错误

✗ 要是你不来，否则我自己去。
✓ 要是你不来，我就自己去。/ 你最好来，否则我自己去了。
Don't combine 要是 directly with 否则 — 否则 already implies 'if not'. Either use 要是…就 or state the condition + 否则.
✗ 否则努力学习，你不能通过考试。
✓ 如果不努力学习，你就不能通过考试。
否则 goes AFTER the main statement, not at the beginning.
Easily confused: 要是…就 = if A, then B (neutral). 要是…否则 = if NOT A, then bad B (warning). See comparison →

Quick Quiz / 小测验

Warning consequence → 否则 (otherwise you'll catch cold).

Simple if-then → 要是.

Casual 'otherwise' → 不然.

要是…否则/不然… FAQ

When is 要是…否则/不然… tested in HSK 4?

要是…否则/不然… (If... otherwise...) appears in HSK 4 reading comprehension (Q66-85) when texts use complex sentences, in 阅读排序 (Q56-65) where this pattern's connectors signal sentence order, and in listening 段落 (Q26-45) where the speaker uses it to express presents a condition and warns about the negative consequence if it's not met.

What's the most common mistake with 要是…否则/不然…?

要是…就 = if A, then B (neutral). 要是…否则 = if NOT A, then bad B (warning).
