HSK 4 还是 vs 或者

or (questions) vs or (statements)

Comparison / 对比

还是 háishi或者 huòzhě
Used inQuestions: 你喝茶还是咖啡？Statements: 你可以喝茶或者咖啡
ExpectsA choice/answerNo choice needed
Special还是…吧 = had better或者 = perhaps
TypeExclusive or (pick one)Inclusive or (either fine)

Examples / 例句

还是: 你是坐地铁还是打车？
Nǐ shì zuò dìtiě háishi dǎ chē?
Subway or taxi? (choose one)
或者: 周末你可以看电影或者去爬山。
Zhōumò nǐ kěyǐ kàn diànyǐng huòzhě qù páshān.
Weekend: watch a movie or go hiking.
Quick rule: 还是 = or (in QUESTIONS, asking someone to choose). 或者 = or (in STATEMENTS, listing options). The most reliable test: is it a question? → 还是. Is it a statement? → 或者. Exception: 还是…吧 can appear in statements as advice meaning "had better" (还是别去了吧 = you'd better not go).

还是 vs 或者 FAQ

Are 还是 and 或者 interchangeable in HSK 4?

No. Although 还是 (háishi) and 或者 (huòzhě) translate similarly into English, HSK 4 fill-in-the-blank and listening questions test exactly the distinction between them. 还是 = or (in QUESTIONS, asking someone to choose).

How is 还是 vs 或者 tested in HSK 4?

Most often in HSK 4 阅读 (reading) Part 1 选词填空 (Q46-55), where you choose the correct word for a blank in a sentence. Also appears in 听力 (listening) where the speaker uses one but the printed answer paraphrases with the other. Master both 还是 and 或者 collocations to lock in these points.

What's the quickest way to remember 还是 vs 或者?

还是 = or (in QUESTIONS, asking someone to choose). 或者 = or (in STATEMENTS, listing options). The most reliable test: is it a question? → 还是. Is it a statement? → 或者. Exception: 还是…吧 can appear in statements as advice meaning "had better" (还是别去了吧 = you'd better not go). Practice 3-5 fill-in-blank questions in our HSK 4 mock exams to lock the distinction.
