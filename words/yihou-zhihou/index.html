HSK 4 以后 vs 之后

after (general future) vs after (specific event)

Comparison / 对比

以后 yǐhòu之后 zhīhòu
ScopeGeneral future: from now onAfter specific event
Standalone以后要注意 (in the future)Rarely standalone
After event吃饭以后 = 吃饭之后Same meaning here
RegisterNeutralSlightly more formal

Examples / 例句

以后: 以后你要更加努力。
Yǐhòu nǐ yào gèngjiā nǔlì.
In the future, work harder.
之后: 毕业之后，他去了上海工作。
Bìyè zhīhòu, tā qù le Shànghǎi gōngzuò.
After graduating, he went to Shanghai.
Quick rule: Both mean after [event]. Only 以后 can stand alone meaning in the future. 之后 always needs a preceding event.

以后 vs 之后 FAQ

Are 以后 and 之后 interchangeable in HSK 4?

No. Although 以后 (yǐhòu) and 之后 (zhīhòu) translate similarly into English, HSK 4 fill-in-the-blank and listening questions test exactly the distinction between them. Both mean after [event].

How is 以后 vs 之后 tested in HSK 4?

Most often in HSK 4 阅读 (reading) Part 1 选词填空 (Q46-55), where you choose the correct word for a blank in a sentence. Also appears in 听力 (listening) where the speaker uses one but the printed answer paraphrases with the other. Master both 以后 and 之后 collocations to lock in these points.

What's the quickest way to remember 以后 vs 之后?

Both mean after [event]. Only 以后 can stand alone meaning in the future. 之后 always needs a preceding event. Practice 3-5 fill-in-blank questions in our HSK 4 mock exams to lock the distinction.
