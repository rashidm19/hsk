HSK 4 于是 vs 因此

so/then (narrative) vs therefore (logical) — both mean 'so' but feel different

Comparison / 对比

于是 yúshì因此 yīncǐ
TypeSequential: then, so (story flow)Causal: therefore, as a result
RegisterNarrative / storytellingFormal / argumentative
FocusWhat happened NEXTLogical CONSEQUENCE
Replaceable by然后 (then)所以 (so/therefore)

Examples / 例句

于是: 天黑了，于是我们决定回家。
Tiān hēi le, yúshì wǒmen juédìng huí jiā.
It got dark, so we decided to go home. (narrative sequence)
因此: 他没有认真复习，因此没有通过考试。
Tā méiyǒu rènzhēn fùxí, yīncǐ méiyǒu tōngguò kǎoshì.
He didn't review seriously; therefore he didn't pass. (logical cause-effect)
Quick rule: 于是 tells a STORY (this happened, then that happened). 因此 makes an ARGUMENT (because of X, therefore Y). In reading passages: 于是 appears in narratives, 因此 in explanatory/analytical texts.

于是 vs 因此 FAQ

Are 于是 and 因此 interchangeable in HSK 4?

No. Although 于是 (yúshì) and 因此 (yīncǐ) translate similarly into English, HSK 4 fill-in-the-blank and listening questions test exactly the distinction between them. 于是 tells a STORY (this happened, then that happened).

How is 于是 vs 因此 tested in HSK 4?

Most often in HSK 4 阅读 (reading) Part 1 选词填空 (Q46-55), where you choose the correct word for a blank in a sentence. Also appears in 听力 (listening) where the speaker uses one but the printed answer paraphrases with the other. Master both 于是 and 因此 collocations to lock in these points.

What's the quickest way to remember 于是 vs 因此?

于是 tells a STORY (this happened, then that happened). 因此 makes an ARGUMENT (because of X, therefore Y). In reading passages: 于是 appears in narratives, 因此 in explanatory/analytical texts. Practice 3-5 fill-in-blank questions in our HSK 4 mock exams to lock the distinction.
