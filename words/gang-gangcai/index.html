HSK 4 刚 vs 刚才

just (adverb before verb) vs just now (time word)

Comparison / 对比

刚 gāng刚才 gāngcái
FunctionAdverb: goes before verbTime noun: can be subject/object modifier
PositionMust be before verb: 刚到Flexible: 刚才的话 / 我刚才说的
With 的Cannot: ×刚的事Can: 刚才的事 (the thing from just now)
Time feelEmphasizes 'just' (very recent action)Points to a moment 'just now'

Examples / 例句

刚: 我刚到公司，还没开始工作。
Wǒ gāng dào gōngsī, hái méi kāishǐ gōngzuò.
I just arrived at the company. (刚 before verb 到)
刚才: 刚才那个人是谁？
Gāngcái nàge rén shì shéi?
Who was that person just now? (刚才 as time word modifying noun)
Quick rule: 刚 = adverb, must go right before a verb (刚吃, 刚到). 刚才 = time noun, can modify nouns (刚才的电话) or stand alone as a time reference. Test: can you put 的 after it? Yes → 刚才. No → 刚.

刚 vs 刚才 FAQ

Are 刚 and 刚才 interchangeable in HSK 4?

No. Although 刚 (gāng) and 刚才 (gāngcái) translate similarly into English, HSK 4 fill-in-the-blank and listening questions test exactly the distinction between them. 刚 = adverb, must go right before a verb (刚吃, 刚到).

How is 刚 vs 刚才 tested in HSK 4?

Most often in HSK 4 阅读 (reading) Part 1 选词填空 (Q46-55), where you choose the correct word for a blank in a sentence. Also appears in 听力 (listening) where the speaker uses one but the printed answer paraphrases with the other. Master both 刚 and 刚才 collocations to lock in these points.

What's the quickest way to remember 刚 vs 刚才?

刚 = adverb, must go right before a verb (刚吃, 刚到). 刚才 = time noun, can modify nouns (刚才的电话) or stand alone as a time reference. Test: can you put 的 after it? Yes → 刚才. No → 刚. Practice 3-5 fill-in-blank questions in our HSK 4 mock exams to lock the distinction.
