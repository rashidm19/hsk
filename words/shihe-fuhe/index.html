HSK 4 适合 vs 符合

to suit (person/preference) vs to conform (rule/standard)

Comparison / 对比

适合 shìhé符合 fúhé
Object typePeople, situations, occasionsRules, standards, requirements, expectations
SenseSubjective fit / suitabilityObjective conformity / matching
Common collocates适合你 / 适合这个场合 / 适合学习符合规定 / 符合要求 / 符合标准 / 符合条件
Negation不适合 = doesn't suit不符合 = doesn't meet (formal sense)

Examples / 例句

适合: 这件衣服很适合你。
Zhè jiàn yīfu hěn shìhé nǐ.
This dress suits you well. (subjective fit)
符合: 你的回答不符合要求。
Nǐ de huídá bù fúhé yāoqiú.
Your answer doesn't meet the requirements. (objective standard)
Quick rule: 适合 = is a good match for someone or some situation (taste, preference, occasion). 符合 = matches an objective standard, rule, or expectation. If you can ask 'fits whom/what occasion?' → 适合. If you can ask 'meets what standard?' → 符合. HSK 4 frequently tests this in fill-in-the-blank questions.

Quick Quiz / 小测验

这份工作很他的兴趣。
Personal interest/preference → 适合. (We don't say 符合兴趣.)
他的条件完全公司的要求。
Meeting company requirements (objective standard) → 符合.
这个时间你吗？
Whether the time suits the listener (personal convenience) → 适合.

适合 vs 符合 FAQ

Are 适合 and 符合 interchangeable in HSK 4?

No. Although 适合 (shìhé) and 符合 (fúhé) translate similarly into English, HSK 4 fill-in-the-blank and listening questions test exactly the distinction between them. 适合 = is a good match for someone or some situation (taste, preference, occasion).

How is 适合 vs 符合 tested in HSK 4?

Most often in HSK 4 阅读 (reading) Part 1 选词填空 (Q46-55), where you choose the correct word for a blank in a sentence. Also appears in 听力 (listening) where the speaker uses one but the printed answer paraphrases with the other. Master both 适合 and 符合 collocations to lock in these points.

What's the quickest way to remember 适合 vs 符合?

适合 = is a good match for someone or some situation (taste, preference, occasion). 符合 = matches an objective standard, rule, or expectation. If you can ask 'fits whom/what occasion?' → 适合. If you can ask 'meets what standard?' → 符合. HSK 4 frequently tests this in fill-in-the-blank questions. Practice 3-5 fill-in-blank questions in our HSK 4 mock exams to lock the distinction.
