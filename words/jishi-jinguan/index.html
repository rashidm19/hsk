HSK 4 即使 vs 尽管

even if (hypothetical) vs despite (factual)

Comparison / 对比

即使 jíshǐ尽管 jǐnguǎn
RealityHypothetical: may not be trueFactual: already true
MeaningEven if (假设): hasn't happened yetDespite (让步): already happened
Pair with即使…也…尽管…但是/还是…
Example即使下雨我也去 (even if it rains)尽管下着雨他还是去了 (despite the rain)

Examples / 例句

即使: 即使明天下雪，我也要去上班。
Jíshǐ míngtiān xià xuě, wǒ yě yào qù shàng bān.
Even if it snows tomorrow, I'll still go to work. (hypothetical — hasn't snowed yet)
尽管: 尽管外面下着大雨，他还是出门了。
Jǐnguǎn wàimiàn xià zhe dà yǔ, tā háishì chū mén le.
Despite the heavy rain outside, he still went out. (factual — it IS raining)
Quick rule: 即使 = EVEN IF (hypothetical, 假设 — the situation has NOT happened yet or may not happen). 尽管 = DESPITE (factual, 事实 — the situation IS true or HAS happened). Check: has it happened? Yes → 尽管. Not yet / might not → 即使. This is one of the most tested distinctions at HSK 4.

即使 vs 尽管 FAQ

Are 即使 and 尽管 interchangeable in HSK 4?

No. Although 即使 (jíshǐ) and 尽管 (jǐnguǎn) translate similarly into English, HSK 4 fill-in-the-blank and listening questions test exactly the distinction between them. 即使 = EVEN IF (hypothetical, 假设 — the situation has NOT happened yet or may not happen).

How is 即使 vs 尽管 tested in HSK 4?

Most often in HSK 4 阅读 (reading) Part 1 选词填空 (Q46-55), where you choose the correct word for a blank in a sentence. Also appears in 听力 (listening) where the speaker uses one but the printed answer paraphrases with the other. Master both 即使 and 尽管 collocations to lock in these points.

What's the quickest way to remember 即使 vs 尽管?

即使 = EVEN IF (hypothetical, 假设 — the situation has NOT happened yet or may not happen). 尽管 = DESPITE (factual, 事实 — the situation IS true or HAS happened). Check: has it happened? Yes → 尽管.
