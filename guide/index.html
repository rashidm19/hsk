HSK 4 Study Guide / HSK4 备考指南

Everything you need to know about the HSK 4 exam — current format (2010 syllabus, 100 questions) and the upcoming 2025 syllabus update (effective July 2026).

HSK 4 备考指南 / HSK4 学习计划 — HSK 4 考试结构、评分标准、HSK3→HSK4 能力跨越分析、复习路线图、HSK4 任务情景清单（30 个交际任务）。基于 2025 年 11 月发布的新版 HSK 考试大纲。

Two formats to know: The current HSK 4 (used through June 2026) is based on the 2010 syllabus and follows the 45 + 40 + 15 question structure shown below — this is the format our 14 mock exams cover. The revised HSK 4 (《新版HSK考试大纲》, published November 2025, effective July 2026) keeps a similar overall scale but expands the writing section to include paragraph composition. If you're sitting the test before July 2026, prepare for the current format. See the full breakdown of every change in our New vs Old HSK 4 comparison.

Each section is scored independently out of 100 points. Your total score is the sum of all three sections (max 300). You need at least 180 points total (60%) to pass. There is no minimum per-section score required.

HSK 3 → HSK 4: 5 Ability Thresholds to Cross / 五大能力跨越

Moving from HSK 3 to HSK 4 isn't just about learning more words. The exam expects qualitative improvements in five dimensions:

Dimension HSK 3 Level HSK 4 Requirement
Complexity 简单 — Simple topics 有一定复杂度 — Some complexity
Fluency 能简单介绍 — Can simply introduce 能比较流利地介绍 — Can introduce fairly fluently
Writing 写出一两句话 — Write 1-2 sentences 写出一段话 — Write a paragraph
Reading 看懂简单小短文 — Understand simple short texts 看懂一般性短文 — Understand general short texts
Thinking Describe facts Express 感受 (feelings) and 看法 (opinions)

The biggest leap is in thinking depth: HSK 3 only requires describing facts, while HSK 4 expects you to express your own feelings and opinions. This affects both writing and speaking preparation.

Recommended Study Plan / 备考计划

A 3-month study plan for learners who have passed HSK 3 and want to prepare for HSK 4. Adjust the timeline based on your daily study hours.

  • Month 1 — Foundation
    Vocabulary & Grammar Building
    Learn 15-20 new vocabulary words per day using our vocabulary tool. Focus on high-frequency words first. Study 2-3 grammar points per week using our grammar guide. Start with 把字句, 被动句, and comparison patterns.
  • Month 2 — Integration
    Reading, Listening & Mock Exams
    Take your first 2-3 mock exams to identify weak areas. Practice listening with Chinese podcasts or videos (15 min/day). Read short articles on HSK 4 topics. Focus on the 30 task scenarios — try writing a short paragraph for each.
  • Month 3 — Exam Simulation
    Full Mock Exams & Weak-Spot Review
    Take 2-14 mock exams per week under timed conditions. Review all wrong answers and identify patterns. Revisit vocabulary words you keep getting wrong. Practice writing paragraphs with a 25-minute timer. Aim to score consistently above 70% before exam day.
  • Final Week
    Review & Confidence
    Do one final full mock exam. Review your vocabulary mastery progress. Re-read grammar notes for any patterns you're still unsure about. Get good sleep — test stamina matters for a 105-minute exam. You've got this!
