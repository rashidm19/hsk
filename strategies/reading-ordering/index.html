HSK 4 Test-Taking Strategy

HSK 4 Sentence Ordering / 排序

Find the first sentence in 5 seconds — connectors do the rest

Section: Reading Part 2 / 阅读第二部分
Questions: Q56-65
Format: 10 questions, 3 sentences per question to be ordered as ABC

HSK 4 reading Part 2 gives you three sentences (labelled A, B, C) and asks you to put them in the correct order. Once you find sentence #1, the other two usually self-arrange via connectors. This section is about pattern recognition, not deep reading.

5 Strategies for HSK 4 Sentence Ordering / 5 个应试策略

1

The first sentence usually has a SUBJECT noun (not a pronoun)

Sentence #1 introduces the topic. It typically starts with a full noun (李教授/这家饭店/中国的茶文化), NOT with 他/她/它/这/那. Eliminate sentences starting with pronouns from the #1 candidate list.

Example A 他后来成了一名优秀的演员。 B 王明小时候很普通。 C 但是父母从不放弃他。 → B starts with full name 王明, A & C start with 他/父母. So B = #1.
2

Connector words tell you the position

Sentence-initial connectors: 因为/虽然/尽管 → usually first or second (introduce a condition). 所以/因此/于是 → after a cause. 而且/并且 → after another point. 但是/不过/可是 → after a contrast. 后来/最后 → near the end.

Example If sentence A starts with 所以, A is NOT first. If B starts with 因为, B comes BEFORE the sentence with 所以.
3

Time markers chain by chronology

看到 时间词: 以前/小时候 → early; 现在/最近 → middle; 后来/最后/将来 → late. Order the sentences by the time they describe.

Example A 他现在是公司的经理。 B 小时候他成绩并不好。 C 后来他努力学习，考上了大学。 → BCA (childhood → improved → present).
4

Pronouns refer back — find the antecedent

If sentence X says '他/它/这' but the noun it refers to is in sentence Y, then Y comes before X. This is the most reliable rule when no connectors are present.

Example A 它的味道又香又甜。 B 这种水果叫芒果。 C 是热带地区常见的水果。 → 它 in A refers to 芒果 from B. B introduces, A continues. Order = BAC or BCA.
5

Use process-of-elimination on the 6 possible orders

Only 6 orderings exist: ABC, ACB, BAC, BCA, CAB, CBA. Identify sentence #1, that eliminates 4 orders. Identify sentence #3 (often ends with a conclusion/result/lesson), that nails the answer.

Example If you're sure B is #1: only BAC or BCA remain. Pick whichever has a connector flow that makes sense.

⚠ Common HSK 4 Mistakes to Avoid / 常见错误

  • Picking sentence with 'introduction feel' as first without checking pronouns — pronouns are the killer.
  • Trying to translate every sentence to English first — wastes 30 seconds per question.
  • Over-thinking when the first 2 sentences are obvious — sometimes you just need to lock those in and the third is forced.

Practice Questions / 练习题

Try these 3 practice items in HSK 4 format. Apply the strategies above and check your reasoning.

Q1
Arrange in correct order:
  • A 我们经常一起去图书馆学习
  • B 还参加了同一个学习小组
  • C 上大学的时候我和小李是同班同学
Why: C introduces context (上大学时) with full nouns 我和小李. A continues with 我们 (refers to me + 小李). B adds 还 (and also). Order: setup → first activity → second activity.
Q2
Arrange in correct order:
  • A 但他从来都不抱怨，反而很快乐
  • B 我的同事张师傅每天工作十几个小时
  • C 对生活充满了热情
Why: B introduces 张师傅 with full noun. A starts with 但 (contrast/turning, must follow B). C continues with 对 referring back, completing the description.
Q3
Arrange in correct order:
  • A 虽然这里的房子不太漂亮
  • B 离地铁站也很近，交通方便
  • C 但是房间很大，光线又好
Why: 虽然 (A) sets up concession. 但是 (C) completes the contrast. B adds another advantage with 也. Pattern: 虽然…但是…，也….
