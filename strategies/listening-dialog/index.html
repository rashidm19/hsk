HSK 4 Test-Taking Strategy

HSK 4 Short Dialogues / 短对话

The third line is the question — predict it from the options

Section: Listening Part 2 / 听力第二部分
Questions: Q11-25
Format: 15 two-line dialogues, played once

HSK 4 listening Part 2 features short two-line dialogues followed by a question about what was said. The four printed options are your roadmap: scan them in 5 seconds and predict what's being asked. This section accounts for 15 of the 45 listening marks.

5 Strategies for HSK 4 Short Dialogues / 5 个应试策略

1

Read the 4 options BEFORE the audio

Options reveal the topic. If you see A 医院 B 银行 C 邮局 D 学校 → the question will be 'where' (在哪儿/去哪儿). If you see A 高兴 B 难过 C 失望 D 紧张 → the question is 'how does X feel' (心情怎么样).

Example Options: A 医生 B 司机 C 老师 D 演员 → question will be 男的/女的是做什么的 (his/her job).
2

The second speaker usually holds the answer

In 70%+ of HSK 4 Part 2 dialogues, the answer comes from the SECOND speaker's reply, not the first speaker's question. Focus your listening attention on speaker 2.

Example A: 你怎么看起来这么累？ B: 我昨天加班到深夜。 → Question: 男的为什么累？ Answer: 加班 (from speaker 2).
3

Memorize the 8 high-frequency question stems

HSK 4 recycles these stems: 男/女的最可能是…？根据对话，可以知道…？女的觉得…怎么样？他们最可能在哪儿？女的让男的做什么？男的为什么…？ — knowing the stem before you hear it doubles your speed.

Example Hear '在哪儿' → location options. Hear '为什么' → reason options. Hear '做什么' → action/profession options.
4

Job & relationship clues = profession questions

If you hear words like 病人, 病房, 处方 → 医生. 学生, 作业, 课文 → 老师. 报道, 采访, 新闻 → 记者. 乘客, 路线, 行李 → 司机/导游. Build a profession-vocabulary mental map.

Example Audio mentions 病人 + 检查 + 处方 → speaker is 医生 even if the word 医生 never appears.
5

The wrongest-looking option is often a trap word from the audio

HSK 4 distractors are usually words that DID appear in the audio but aren't the answer. If an option uses an exact word from the dialogue, double-check before picking it.

Example Dialogue mentions 'sandwich' as something they didn't eat. Option C says 'they ate a sandwich' — wrong, but tempts you because of word recognition.

⚠ Common HSK 4 Mistakes to Avoid / 常见错误

  • Listening to the first speaker too intently — you've already used your attention budget by the time speaker 2 (the answer) arrives.
  • Picking an option just because it has the same word as the audio — distractors are designed this way.
  • Skipping the option scan — you'll be hearing meaning blindly without knowing what to extract.

Practice Questions / 练习题

Try these 3 practice items in HSK 4 format. Apply the strategies above and check your reasoning.

Q1
Audio (transcript): 男：明天的会议你准备好了吗？ 女：还差一份材料，下班前能弄完。
女的为什么还没准备好？
Why: 女的 says 还差一份材料 = still missing one piece of material. Direct lift from speaker 2.
Q2
Audio (transcript): 男：服务员，请问厕所在哪儿？ 女：往前走，到电梯左边就是。
男的最可能在哪儿？
Why: He calls 服务员 (waiter) and asks for the bathroom — most likely in a 餐厅 (restaurant).
Q3
Audio (transcript): 女：你最近怎么不去打篮球了？ 男：膝盖受伤了，医生让我休息两个月。
男的为什么不打篮球？
Why: 膝盖受伤了 = knee injured = 受伤了. Direct paraphrase from speaker 2.
