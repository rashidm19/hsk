Why HSK 4 strategies matter / 为什么需要应试策略

Two HSK 4 candidates can have the same vocabulary, but the one who understands the question patterns will score 15-30 points higher. The HSK 4 exam recycles question stems, distractors, and traps across years. Once you can read the test designer's intent, your accuracy and speed both jump.

Recommended HSK 4 Study Order / 学习顺序建议

  1. Week 1-2: Read High-Frequency Signal Words first — applies to all listening sections.
  2. Week 2-3: Listening strategies (Parts 1, 2, 3) — apply on mock exams 01-04.
  3. Week 3-4: Reading strategies (fill-blank, ordering, comprehension) — apply on mock exams 05-08.
  4. Week 4-5: Writing strategies (construction + picture templates) — these are highest-yield late-stage gains.
  5. Week 5-6: Take 4 timed mock exams (09-12) under exam conditions, applying all strategies together.
