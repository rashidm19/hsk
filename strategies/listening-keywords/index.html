HSK 4 Test-Taking Strategy

HSK 4 High-Frequency Signal Words / 高频信号词

60 signal words that flag the answer in HSK 4 listening — train your ear to catch them

Section: All Listening Sections / 听力全部分
Questions: Q1-45
Format: Strategic vocabulary that decodes meaning before you hear it

HSK 4 listening has hidden 'signal words' that almost always mark where the answer lies. Train your ear to flag these in real time and your listening accuracy jumps without learning a single new noun. This page lists the 6 categories of signal words tested in every HSK 4 exam.

6 Strategies for HSK 4 High-Frequency Signal Words / 6 个应试策略

1

Turning words (转折词) — answer comes AFTER

When you hear 但是 / 可是 / 不过 / 然而 / 却 / 其实 / 反而, the speaker is about to flip the meaning. The CORRECT answer is what follows, not what precedes.

Example 听到: '我以前不喜欢运动，但是现在每天都跑步。' → 答案焦点在后半 (现在每天跑步).
2

Causal words (因果词) — link cause and effect

因为 / 由于 / 所以 / 因此 / 于是 / 结果 — listen for these to answer 'why' (为什么) questions. The reason follows 因为/由于; the result follows 所以/因此.

Example 因为下雨，所以比赛取消了。 → 比赛取消的原因 = 下雨.
3

Conclusion words (总结词) — answer the main idea

总之 / 总的来说 / 一句话 / 由此可见 / 可见 — these signal the speaker's overall conclusion. They almost always carry the main-idea answer for monologue passages.

Example 段落最后: '总之，做事要有耐心。' → 主要想告诉我们 = 做事要有耐心.
4

Suggestion words (建议词) — answer 'lesson/advice' questions

应该 / 必须 / 一定要 / 最好 / 千万不要 / 要 / 不要. The 'lesson' or 'advice' the speaker wants you to take is wrapped in these words.

Example 段落最后: '我们应该多锻炼，少熬夜。' → 道理 = 应该锻炼.
5

Emotion words (感情词) — answer 'attitude' questions

高兴/开心/激动 (positive); 难过/失望/伤心/担心 (negative); 紧张/惊讶/吃惊 (surprised). When question asks 心情怎么样 or 觉得怎么样, listen for the emotion-bearing word.

Example 听到: '听到这个消息我真的很激动！' → 心情 = 激动 / 高兴.
6

Negation words (否定词) — flip the meaning

不 / 没 / 没有 / 别 / 不要 / 从来不 / 一点儿也不. A single 不 changes the meaning entirely. The HSK 4 trap is omitting the 不 from the printed sentence.

Example 听到: '我没去过北京。' 印刷: '我去过北京。' → 错 (negation flip).

⚠ Common HSK 4 Mistakes to Avoid / 常见错误

  • Trying to translate every Chinese word to English while listening — you'll miss the signal word.
  • Picking an option just because you heard a similar word, without checking the signal context.
  • Ignoring negation words because they're short — 不/没 is a one-syllable, high-impact word.

Practice Questions / 练习题

Try these 3 practice items in HSK 4 format. Apply the strategies above and check your reasoning.

Q1
Audio (transcript): 我以前觉得这家饭店的菜不好吃，但是上次去尝了他们的新菜，发现味道很不错。
说话人觉得这家饭店：
Why: Turning word 但是 → answer is in the second half. 'New dishes are pretty good' = 现在很好吃.
Q2
Audio (transcript): 由于天气原因，今天下午的足球比赛被取消了。
比赛为什么被取消？
Why: Causal word 由于 followed by 天气原因. Direct cause-extraction.
Q3
Audio (transcript): 总之，学外语最重要的是坚持，每天学一点儿，时间长了就会有很大的进步。
这段话主要想告诉我们：
Why: Conclusion word 总之 + 最重要的是坚持. The main-idea answer follows the summary signal.
