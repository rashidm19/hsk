What the HSK 4 Writing Section Tests / HSK4 写作考什么

The HSK 4 writing section (写作) is the only part of the exam where you produce Chinese from scratch — there are no answer options to choose from. Under the 2026 syllabus it has 6 questions in 25 minutes, split into two task types:

第一部分 · Part 1

看图造句

Questions 65–69 · 5 items · picture sentences

You are shown a picture and one word. Write a single complete, grammatically correct sentence that uses the word and fits the picture.

Official sample — question 65
🖼️ Picture: a customer paying at a shop counter
词语：结账
参考答案：我没带现金，请问能用信用卡结账吗？
第二部分 · Part 2

写短文

Question 70 · 1 item · short essay

You are given a topic and write a short essay of at least 80 characters (不少于80字) — a connected paragraph with an opening, details, and a closing.

Official sample — question 70
请写一篇不少于 80 字 的短文，介绍一下你最难忘的一次旅行。

Practise this exact task type in the 写短文 trainer below.

2026 update: The new HSK syllabus (《新版HSK考试大纲》, published December 2025, effective July 2026) changed this section. It was renamed 书写 → 写作, the old 排词成句 word-ordering task was removed, and 写短文 (short essay) was added. HSK 4 now has 70 questions in total; writing is questions 65–70. Practice material that shows 完成句子 as questions 86–95 reflects the older format.

Scoring & Common Deductions / 评分与常见扣分

The official structure document does not publish exact point values, but the marking logic is consistent: did you complete the task, and is your Chinese accurate? Here is what raises and lowers your score.

第一部分 看图造句

  • A complete sentence that clearly uses the given word
  • Natural word order and correct characters
  • Not using the given word, or using it wrongly
  • An incomplete sentence or just a phrase
  • Wrong word order (把/被 position, adverbs, time/place)
  • Wrong or missing characters; missing 了/过/着
  • Missing or half-width punctuation (use ，。？)

第二部分 写短文

  • At least 80 characters, on topic, with clear structure
  • Sentences linked with connectors; varied patterns
  • Fewer than 80 characters — the biggest single deduction
  • Off-topic, or ignoring part of the prompt
  • One long run-on instead of organised sentences
  • Repeated grammar errors and wrong characters
  • No opening or no closing

Golden rule: a simple, correct sentence always scores higher than an ambitious, broken one. Write what you are sure of.

Writing Strategy & Key Grammar / 写作技巧与核心语法

1
看图造句：先定句型，再放词语

Decide the sentence pattern first (把字句? 比较句? a plain S+V+O?), then slot the given word into it. Keep it short — one clear clause is enough. Read it back: if it sounds unnatural, fix the order.

2
写短文：先想结构，再写内容

Spend 30 seconds planning opening → middle → closing before writing. A clear structure prevents rambling and earns more marks than extra length. Aim a little over 80 characters so you are safely past the minimum.

3
用你确定会的词，检查"了/过/着"和标点

Writing is not the place to gamble on half-remembered vocabulary. After finishing, scan for the most common deductions: missing aspect markers (了/过/着) and punctuation — Chinese uses the full-width comma "，", not ",".

The grammar patterns most worth mastering for writing: 把字句, 被字句, 比较句, 复句连接词 (虽然…但是, 不但…而且, 因为…所以), and adverb placement. Solid sentence accuracy also comes from word-order practice.

Frequently Asked Questions / 常见问题

What does the HSK 4 writing (写作) section test?
Under the 2026 syllabus, HSK 4 写作 has 6 questions in 25 minutes, in two parts. Part 1 (看图造句, Q65–69, 5 items): you see a picture and a word and write one complete sentence using that word. Part 2 (写短文, Q70, 1 item): you write a short essay of at least 80 characters on a given topic. It is the only section where you produce Chinese from scratch.
Did the HSK 4 writing section change for 2026?
Yes. The new HSK syllabus (《新版HSK考试大纲》, published December 2025, effective July 2026) changed it significantly. The section was renamed 书写 → 写作, the old 排词成句 word-ordering task was removed, and 写短文 was added. HSK 4 now has 70 questions in total, and writing is questions 65–70. Older practice material showing 完成句子 as Q86–95 reflects the previous format.
How long does the 写短文 essay need to be?
At least 80 characters (不少于80字). The official sample asks you to introduce your most memorable trip. Aim for a little above 80 characters so you are safely over the minimum, with a clear opening, middle, and closing.
How is HSK 4 writing scored — and what causes point deductions?
The published exam structure does not list exact point values. In practice, examiners check whether you completed the task and wrote accurate Chinese. Common deductions: not using the given word in Part 1, incomplete sentences, wrong word order, wrong or missing characters, wrong punctuation, and — for Part 2 — writing fewer than 80 characters, going off-topic, or having no clear structure.
How should I practise HSK 4 writing?
Use the two trainers on this page. For 看图造句, write your own sentence for each word, then compare it with the model sentence. For 写短文, pick a prompt, write 80+ characters using the structure helper, then check it against the model paragraph and the self-check list. Practise a little every day — writing is a productive skill that improves with repetition.
