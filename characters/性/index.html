How to write 性 (xìng) — Stroke Order, Radical & Practice

性
xìng sex, nature, character; suffix converting a verb to an adjective 8 strokes · Radical 忄 (heart) · HSK 4 required writing character
Quick Answer

性 (xìng) means sex, nature, character; suffix converting a verb to an adjective. It is written in 8 strokes, with the radical 忄 (heart), and is one of the 150 HSK 4 required writing characters (rank #7 by appearance in HSK 4 vocabulary).

Pronunciation & meaning

Pronounced xìng.

  • sex
  • nature
  • character
  • suffix converting a verb to an adjective

Decomposition

性 breaks down into 2 components arranged as Left + Right. Recognizing the components makes the character easier to remember and write.

性 =
忄 left heart
生 right life

Radical

The radical of 性 is 忄 — meaning heart. Radicals are the indexing components used in Chinese dictionaries; they often hint at a character's broad meaning category.

How the character is built

性 is a phono-semantic compound. The semantic component 忄 (heart) carries the meaning, while the phonetic component 生 originally indicated the sound.

HSK 4 Words Containing 性

男性 nánxìng male; man (formal)
这款手表适合男性佩戴。
Zhè kuǎn shǒubiǎo shìhé nánxìng pèidài.
This watch is suitable for men to wear.
女性 nǚxìng female; woman (formal)
越来越多的女性成为企业家。
Yuèláiyuè duō de nǚxìng chéngwéi qǐyèjiā.
More and more women are becoming entrepreneurs.
性 xìng (suffix: -ness, -ity)
这个问题具有普遍性。
Zhège wèntí jùyǒu pǔbiàn xìng.
This issue is universal in nature.
性别 xìngbié gender; sex
表格上需要填写性别。
Biǎogé shàng xūyào tiánxiě xìngbié.
You need to fill in your gender on the form.
性格 xìnggé personality; character; nature
她的性格很开朗。
Tā de xìnggé hěn kāilǎng.
She has an outgoing personality.
