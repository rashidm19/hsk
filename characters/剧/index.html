这部电视剧非常好看。
Zhè bù diànshìjù fēicháng hǎokàn.

我们明天去看话剧。
Wǒmen míngtiān qù kàn huàjù.
We're going to see a play tomorrow.

京剧是中国的国粹。
Jīngjù shì Zhōngguó de guócuì.
Peking Opera is a national treasure of China.

Jīnwǎn wǒmen qù jùyuàn kàn yǎnchū.
We're going to the theater to see a show tonight.

About the character 剧

剧 (jù) means drama; play; severe. It is one of the 150 characters HSK 4 expects you to write from memory. Practice the stroke order until it feels automatic — most learners need 5–10 successful traces before a character "sticks".
