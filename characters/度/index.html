How to write 度 (dù) — Stroke Order, Radical & Practice

度
dù degree, system; manner; to consider 9 strokes · Radical 广 (broad) · HSK 4 required writing character
Quick Answer

度 (dù) means degree, system; manner; to consider. It is written in 9 strokes, with the radical 广 (broad), and is one of the 150 HSK 4 required writing characters (rank #11 by appearance in HSK 4 vocabulary).

Pronunciation & meaning

Pronounced dù.

  • degree
  • system
  • manner
  • to consider

Stroke Order & Practice

Decomposition

度 breaks down into 3 components arranged as Upper-left envelops Inner. Recognizing the components makes the character easier to remember and write.

度 = 广 outer broad
廿 inner twenty
又 part and

Radical

The radical of 度 is 广 — meaning broad. Radicals are the indexing components used in Chinese dictionaries; they often hint at a character's broad meaning category.

Other HSK 4 characters that share this radical: 庭座广.

How the character is built

度 is an ideographic compound — its meaning is suggested by the combination of its parts rather than by sound. Twenty 廿 people 又 meeting inside a building 广 to deliberate.

HSK 4 Words Containing 度

度假 dùjià to go on holiday; to vacation
我们打算去海边度假。
Wǒmen dǎsuàn qù hǎibiān dùjià.
We plan to go on vacation at the beach.
摄氏度 shèshìdù degree Celsius
今天气温三十摄氏度。
Jīntiān qìwēn sānshí shèshìdù.
The temperature today is 30 degrees Celsius.
速度 sùdù speed
请降低行驶速度。
Qǐng jiàngdī xíngshǐ sùdù.
Please reduce your driving speed.
态度 tàidù attitude; manner
服务员的态度很好。
Fúwùyuán de tàidù hěn hǎo.
The waiter's attitude was very good.
温度 wēndù temperature
请把空调温度调低一点。
Qǐng bǎ kōngtiáo wēndù tiáo dī yìdiǎn.
Please turn the AC temperature down a bit.
