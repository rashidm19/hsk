How to write 队 (duì) — Stroke Order, Radical & Practice

队
duì team, group, band, army unit; measure word for groups of people 4 strokes · Radical 阝 (place) · HSK 4 required writing character
Quick Answer

队 (duì) means team, group, band, army unit; measure word for groups of people. It is written in 4 strokes, with the radical 阝 (place), and is one of the 150 HSK 4 required writing characters (rank #12 by appearance in HSK 4 vocabulary).

Pronunciation & meaning

Pronounced duì.

  • team
  • group
  • band
  • army unit
  • measure word for groups of people

Decomposition

队 breaks down into 2 components arranged as Left + Right. Recognizing the components makes the character easier to remember and write.

队 =
阝 left place
人 right man

Radical

The radical of 队 is 阝 — meaning place. Radicals are the indexing components used in Chinese dictionaries; they often hint at a character's broad meaning category.

Other HSK 4 characters that share this radical: 部际随.

How the character is built

队 is an ideographic compound — its meaning is suggested by the combination of its parts rather than by sound. A group of people 人 gathered in one place 阝.

HSK 4 Words Containing 队

队 duì team; line; queue
我们的足球队赢了今天的比赛。
Wǒmen de zúqiú duì yíng le jīntiān de bǐsài.
Our football team won today's match.
队员 duìyuán team member
每个队员都很努力。
Měi gè duìyuán dōu hěn nǔlì.
Every team member works hard.
队长 duìzhǎng team captain; team leader
他被选为队长。
Tā bèi xuǎn wéi duìzhǎng.
He was chosen as the team captain.
排队 páiduì to queue up; to line up
买票要排队。
Mǎi piào yào páiduì.
You have to line up to buy tickets.
球队 qiúduì sports team; ball team
他加入了学校的足球队。
Tā jiārù le xuéxiào de zúqiú duì.
He joined the school football team.
