How to write 各 (gè) — Stroke Order, Radical & Practice

各
gè individual; each, every; all 6 strokes · Radical 口 (mouth) · HSK 4 required writing character
Quick Answer

各 (gè) means individual; each, every; all. It is written in 6 strokes, with the radical 口 (mouth), and is one of the 150 HSK 4 required writing characters (rank #14 by appearance in HSK 4 vocabulary).

Pronunciation & meaning

Pronounced gè.

  • individual
  • each
  • every
  • all

Decomposition

各 breaks down into 2 components arranged as Top + Bottom. Recognizing the components makes the character easier to remember and write.

各 =
夂 top to go
口 bottom mouth

Radical

The radical of 各 is 口 — meaning mouth. Radicals are the indexing components used in Chinese dictionaries; they often hint at a character's broad meaning category.

Other HSK 4 characters that share this radical: 品吹台否另味喊.

How the character is built

各 is an ideographic compound — its meaning is suggested by the combination of its parts rather than by sound. Walking 夂 and talking 口.

HSK 4 Words Containing 各

各 gè each; every; various
各位同学，请安静。
Gèwèi tóngxué, qǐng ānjìng.
Everyone, please be quiet.
各地 gèdì various places; everywhere
全国各地的人都来了。
Quánguó gèdì de rén dōu lái le.
People from all over the country came.
各个 gègè each; every (one)
各个国家的文化不同。
Gègè guójiā de wénhuà bùtóng.
Each country has a different culture.
各位 gèwèi everyone; ladies and gentlemen
各位同学好，请坐。
Gèwèi tóngxué hǎo, qǐng zuò.
Hello everyone, please sit down.
各种 gèzhǒng various kinds; all sorts of
超市里有各种水果。
Chāoshì lǐ yǒu gèzhǒng shuǐguǒ.
The supermarket has all kinds of fruit.
