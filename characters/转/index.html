HSK 4 Words Containing 转

转 zhuǎn to turn; to change direction
到前面的路口向右转。
Dào qiánmiàn de lùkǒu xiàng yòu zhuǎn.
Turn right at the intersection ahead.
转发 zhuǎnfā to forward; to repost
请把这条消息转发给大家。
Qǐng bǎ zhè tiáo xiāoxi zhuǎnfā gěi dàjiā.
Please forward this message to everyone.
转机1 zhuǎnjī to transfer (flights); transit
我们需要在上海转机。
Wǒmen xūyào zài Shànghǎi zhuǎnjī.
We need to transfer flights in Shanghai.

About the character 转

转 (zhuǎn) means to turn; to transfer; to forward. It is one of the 150 characters HSK 4 expects you to write from memory. Practice the stroke order until it feels automatic — most learners need 5–10 successful traces before a character "sticks".
