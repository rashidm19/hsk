How to write 温 (wēn) — Stroke Order, Radical & Practice

温
wēn warm, lukewarm 12 strokes · Radical 氵 (water) · HSK 4 required writing character
Quick Answer

温 (wēn) means warm, lukewarm. It is written in 12 strokes, with the radical 氵 (water), and is one of the 150 HSK 4 required writing characters (rank #6 by appearance in HSK 4 vocabulary).

Pronunciation & meaning

Pronounced wēn.

  • warm
  • lukewarm

Stroke Order & Practice

Click Animate to see the correct stroke order, then Practice to trace it yourself.

Decomposition

温 breaks down into 2 components arranged as Left + Right. Recognizing the components makes the character easier to remember and write.

温 =
氵 left water
昷 right warm

Radical

The radical of 温 is 氵 — meaning water. Radicals are the indexing components used in Chinese dictionaries; they often hint at a character's broad meaning category.

Other HSK 4 characters that share this radical: 济江深油汤流.

How the character is built

温 is a phono-semantic compound. The semantic component 氵 (water) carries the meaning, while the phonetic component 昷 originally indicated the sound.

HSK 4 Words Containing 温

低温 dīwēn low temperature
冬天低温天气要注意保暖。
Dōngtiān dīwēn tiānqì yào zhùyì bǎonuǎn.
Keep warm in cold winter weather.
高温 gāowēn high temperature
夏天的高温让人受不了。
Xiàtiān de gāowēn ràng rén shòu bù liǎo.
The high temperatures in summer are unbearable.
降温 jiàngwēn to cool down; temperature drop
明天要大幅降温。
Míngtiān yào dàfú jiàngwēn.
There will be a big temperature drop tomorrow.
气温 qìwēn air temperature
明天气温会上升。
Míngtiān qìwēn huì shàngshēng.
The temperature will rise tomorrow.
体温 tǐwēn body temperature
护士测量了他的体温。
Hùshi cèliáng le tā de tǐwēn.
The nurse measured his temperature.
温度 wēndù temperature
请把空调温度调低一点。
Qǐng bǎ kōngtiáo wēndù tiáo dī yìdiǎn.
Please turn the AC temperature down a bit.
