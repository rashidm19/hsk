How to write 印 (yìn) — Stroke Order, Radical & Practice

印
yìn print; mark; seal, stamp 5 strokes · Radical 卩 (seal) · HSK 4 required writing character
Quick Answer

印 (yìn) means print; mark; seal, stamp. It is written in 5 strokes, with the radical 卩 (seal), and is one of the 150 HSK 4 required writing characters (rank #19 by appearance in HSK 4 vocabulary).

Pronunciation & meaning

Pronounced yìn.

  • print
  • mark
  • seal
  • stamp

Decomposition

印 breaks down into 1 component arranged as Left + Right. Recognizing the components makes the character easier to remember and write.

印 =
卩 left seal

Radical

The radical of 印 is 卩 — meaning seal. Radicals are the indexing components used in Chinese dictionaries; they often hint at a character's broad meaning category.

How the character is built

印 is an ideographic compound — its meaning is suggested by the combination of its parts rather than by sound. A hand 爪 stamping a seal 卩.

HSK 4 Words Containing 印

打印 dǎyìn to print
请帮我打印这份文件。
Qǐng bāng wǒ dǎyìn zhè fèn wénjiàn.
Please help me print this document.
打印机 dǎyìnjī printer
办公室的打印机坏了。
Bàngōngshì de dǎyìnjī huài le.
The office printer is broken.
复印 fùyìn to photocopy; to duplicate
请帮我复印两份这个文件。
Qǐng bāng wǒ fùyìn liǎng fèn zhège wénjiàn.
Please help me copy this document twice.
印象 yìnxiàng impression
她给我留下了很好的印象。
Tā gěi wǒ liúxia le hěn hǎo de yìnxiàng.
She left me with a very good impression.
