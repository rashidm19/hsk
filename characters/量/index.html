How to write 量 (liàng) — Stroke Order, Radical & Practice

量
liàng measure, volume; amount, quantity 12 strokes · Radical 里 (unit of distance equal to 0.5km) · HSK 4 required writing character
Quick Answer

量 (liàng) means measure, volume; amount, quantity. It is written in 12 strokes, with the radical 里 (unit of distance equal to 0.5km), and is one of the 150 HSK 4 required writing characters (rank #4 by appearance in HSK 4 vocabulary).

Pronunciation & meaning

Pronounced liàng.

  • measure
  • volume
  • amount
  • quantity

Decomposition

量 breaks down into 2 components arranged as Top + Bottom. Recognizing the components makes the character easier to remember and write.

量 =
旦 top dawn
里 bottom unit of distance equal to 0.5km

Radical

The radical of 量 is 里 — meaning unit of distance equal to 0.5km. Radicals are the indexing components used in Chinese dictionaries; they often hint at a character's broad meaning category.

How the character is built

量 is a phono-semantic compound. The semantic component 里 (unit) carries the meaning, while the phonetic component 旦 originally indicated the sound.

HSK 4 Words Containing 量

大量 dàliàng large amount; a lot of
网上有大量的学习资源。
Wǎng shàng yǒu dàliàng de xuéxí zīyuán.
There are a large number of learning resources online.
量 liáng to measure
护士帮我量了体温。
Hùshi bāng wǒ liáng le tǐwēn.
The nurse took my temperature.
商量 shāngliang to discuss; to consult
这件事我们商量一下再决定。
Zhè jiàn shì wǒmen shāngliang yíxià zài juédìng.
Let's discuss this before making a decision.
少量 shǎoliàng small amount; a little
只需要加少量的盐就可以了。
Zhǐ xūyào jiā shǎoliàng de yán jiù kěyǐ le.
Just add a small amount of salt.
数量 shùliàng quantity; amount; number
商品的数量有限。
Shāngpǐn de shùliàng yǒuxiàn.
The quantity of goods is limited.
质量 zhìliàng quality
这个品牌的质量很好。
Zhège pǐnpái de zhìliàng hěn hǎo.
This brand has very good quality.
