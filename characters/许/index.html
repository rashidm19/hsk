HSK 4 Words Containing 许

许多 xǔduō many; numerous
许多学生报名了这次活动。
Xǔduō xuéshēng bàomíng le zhè cì huódòng.
Many students signed up for this activity.
也许 yěxǔ perhaps; maybe
也许他今天不会来了。
Yěxǔ tā jīntiān bú huì lái le.
Maybe he won't come today.
允许 yǔnxǔ to allow; to permit
这里不允许拍照。
Zhèlǐ bù yǔnxǔ pāizhào.
Photography is not allowed here.

About the character 许

许 (xǔ) means to allow; perhaps; to promise. It is one of the 150 characters HSK 4 expects you to write from memory. Practice the stroke order until it feels automatic — most learners need 5–10 successful traces before a character "sticks".
