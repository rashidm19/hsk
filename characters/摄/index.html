About the character 摄

摄 (shè) means to take in; Celsius (as in 摄氏度); to photograph. It is one of the 150 characters HSK 4 expects you to write from memory. Practice the stroke order until it feels automatic — most learners need 5–10 successful traces before a character "sticks".
