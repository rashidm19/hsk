How to write 取 (qǔ) — Stroke Order, Radical & Practice

取
qǔ to take, to receive, to obtain; to select 8 strokes · Radical 又 (and) · HSK 4 required writing character
Quick Answer

取 (qǔ) means to take, to receive, to obtain; to select. It is written in 8 strokes, with the radical 又 (and), and is one of the 150 HSK 4 required writing characters (rank #18 by appearance in HSK 4 vocabulary).

Pronunciation & meaning

Pronounced qǔ.

  • to take
  • to receive
  • to obtain
  • to select

Decomposition

取 breaks down into 2 components arranged as Left + Right. Recognizing the components makes the character easier to remember and write.

取 =
耳 left ear
又 right and

Radical

The radical of 取 is 又 — meaning and. Radicals are the indexing components used in Chinese dictionaries; they often hint at a character's broad meaning category.

Other HSK 4 characters that share this radical: 及反.

How the character is built

取 is an ideographic compound — its meaning is suggested by the combination of its parts rather than by sound. A hand 又 taking the ear 耳 of a fallen enemy.

HSK 4 Words Containing 取

获取 huòqǔ to obtain; to acquire
通过学习可以获取更多知识。
Tōngguò xuéxí kěyǐ huòqǔ gèng duō zhīshi.
Through studying you can acquire more knowledge.
取 qǔ to take; to get; to collect
我去银行取一些钱。
Wǒ qù yínháng qǔ yìxiē qián.
I'm going to the bank to withdraw some money.
取得 qǔdé to obtain; to achieve
经过努力，他取得了好成绩。
Jīngguò nǔlì, tā qǔdé le hǎo chéngjì.
Through hard work, he achieved good results.
取消 qǔxiāo to cancel
因为天气原因，航班被取消了。
Yīnwèi tiānqì yuányīn, hángbān bèi qǔxiāo le.
The flight was cancelled due to weather.
