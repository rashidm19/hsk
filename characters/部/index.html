How to write 部 (bù) — Stroke Order, Radical & Practice

部
bù department, ministry; division, unit; part, section 10 strokes · Radical 阝 (place) · HSK 4 required writing character
Quick Answer

部 (bù) means department, ministry; division, unit; part, section. It is written in 10 strokes, with the radical 阝 (place), and is one of the 150 HSK 4 required writing characters (rank #1 by appearance in HSK 4 vocabulary).

Pronunciation & meaning

Pronounced bù.

  • department
  • ministry
  • division
  • unit
  • part
  • section

Decomposition

部 breaks down into 2 components arranged as Left + Right. Recognizing the components makes the character easier to remember and write.

部 =
咅 left to spit out
阝 right place

Radical

The radical of 部 is 阝 — meaning place. Radicals are the indexing components used in Chinese dictionaries; they often hint at a character's broad meaning category.

Other HSK 4 characters that share this radical: 际随队.

How the character is built

部 is a phono-semantic compound. The semantic component 阝 (place) carries the meaning, while the phonetic component 咅 originally indicated the sound.

HSK 4 Words Containing 部

北部 běibù northern part
哈尔滨在中国北部。
Hā'ěrbīn zài Zhōngguó běibù.
Harbin is in the northern part of China.
部 bù part; section; ministry
这部电影很有意思。
Zhè bù diànyǐng hěn yǒu yìsi.
This movie is very interesting.
部分 bùfen part; portion
这只是问题的一部分。
Zhè zhǐ shì wèntí de yí bùfen.
This is only part of the problem.
部门 bùmén department; division
他在市场部门工作。
Tā zài shìchǎng bùmén gōngzuò.
He works in the marketing department.
东部 dōngbù eastern part
上海在中国的东部。
Shànghǎi zài Zhōngguó de dōngbù.
Shanghai is in the eastern part of China.
南部 nánbù southern part
广州在中国南部。
Guǎngzhōu zài Zhōngguó nánbù.
Guangzhou is in the southern part of China.
全部 quánbù all; everything; the whole
作业全部做完了。
Zuòyè quánbù zuò wán le.
All the homework is done.
西部 xībù western part
中国西部有壮丽的自然风光。
Zhōngguó xībù yǒu zhuànglì de zìrán fēngguāng.
Western China has magnificent natural scenery.
