How to write 美 (měi) — Stroke Order, Radical & Practice

美
měi beautiful, pretty; pleasing 9 strokes · Radical 羊 (sheep) · HSK 4 required writing character
Quick Answer

美 (měi) means beautiful, pretty; pleasing. It is written in 9 strokes, with the radical 羊 (sheep), and is one of the 150 HSK 4 required writing characters (rank #16 by appearance in HSK 4 vocabulary).

Pronunciation & meaning

Pronounced měi.

  • beautiful
  • pretty
  • pleasing

Decomposition

美 breaks down into 2 components arranged as Top + Bottom. Recognizing the components makes the character easier to remember and write.

美 =
羊 top sheep
大 bottom big

Radical

The radical of 美 is 羊 — meaning sheep. Radicals are the indexing components used in Chinese dictionaries; they often hint at a character's broad meaning category.

How the character is built

美 is an ideographic compound — its meaning is suggested by the combination of its parts rather than by sound. A person 大 wearing an elegant crown 羊.

HSK 4 Words Containing 美

美 měi beautiful; pretty
这首歌唱得真美。
Zhè shǒu gē chàng de zhēn měi.
This song was sung beautifully.
美好 měihǎo wonderful; splendid
祝你有一个美好的假期。
Zhù nǐ yǒu yí gè měihǎo de jiàqī.
Wish you a wonderful holiday.
美景 měijǐng beautiful scenery
旅途中我们看到了许多美景。
Lǚtú zhōng wǒmen kàndào le xǔduō měijǐng.
We saw many beautiful scenes during the trip.
美丽 měilì beautiful; gorgeous
她是一个美丽的姑娘。
Tā shì yí gè měilì de gūniang.
She is a beautiful girl.
美食 měishí delicious food; gourmet food
成都有很多美食。
Chéngdū yǒu hěn duō měishí.
Chengdu has lots of delicious food.
