How to write 餐 (cān) — Stroke Order, Radical & Practice

餐
cān to eat, to dine; meal, food 16 strokes · Radical 食 (food) · HSK 4 required writing character
Quick Answer

餐 (cān) means to eat, to dine; meal, food. It is written in 16 strokes, with the radical 食 (food), and is one of the 150 HSK 4 required writing characters (rank #2 by appearance in HSK 4 vocabulary).

Pronunciation & meaning

Pronounced cān.

  • to eat
  • to dine
  • meal
  • food

Decomposition

餐 breaks down into 3 components arranged as Top + Bottom. Recognizing the components makes the character easier to remember and write.

餐 =
歺 top evil
又 bottom and
食 part food

Radical

The radical of 餐 is 食 — meaning food. Radicals are the indexing components used in Chinese dictionaries; they often hint at a character's broad meaning category.

Other HSK 4 characters that share this radical: 食.
