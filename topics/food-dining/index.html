HSK 4 介绍饮食情况 — Food & Dining

Describe food flavors, restaurant experiences, and cooking processes. Goes beyond ordering food (HSK 3) to discussing taste, food culture, and sharing dining experiences.

Official Syllabus Requirement / 大纲要求

能听懂关于食物饮品、就餐情况、菜品制作情况等有一定复杂度的问题或介绍。如饮食味道、种类、特点、餐厅环境、服务、制作过程等。

Scenario Dialogue / 情景对话

Ordering at a restaurant and asking about the dishes. Read it aloud twice: once for meaning, once for fluency — the syllabus tests this task across listening and speaking.

A
服务员，你们店有什么特色菜？
Fúwùyuán, nǐmen diàn yǒu shénme tèsè cài?
Waiter, what are your restaurant's special dishes?
B
我们的烤鸭最有名，皮脆肉嫩，很多客人专门为它来的。
Wǒmen de kǎoyā zuì yǒumíng, pí cuì ròu nèn, hěn duō kèrén zhuānmén wèi tā lái de.
Our roast duck is the most famous — crispy skin, tender meat. Many guests come especially for it.
A
好，来半只烤鸭。这个汤辣不辣？我不太能吃辣。
Hǎo, lái bàn zhī kǎoyā. Zhège tāng là bu là? Wǒ bú tài néng chī là.
Great, half a roast duck please. Is this soup spicy? I can't really eat spicy food.
B
这个是西红柿鸡蛋汤，一点儿都不辣，味道很清淡。
Zhège shì xīhóngshì jīdàn tāng, yìdiǎnr dōu bú là, wèidao hěn qīngdàn.
That is tomato and egg soup — not spicy at all, very light.
A
那再来一份这个汤。对了，能开发票吗？
Nà zài lái yí fèn zhège tāng. Duì le, néng kāi fāpiào ma?
Then one of those soups too. Oh — can you issue a receipt?
B
可以，吃完后凭小票到前台开就行。
Kěyǐ, chī wán hòu píng xiǎopiào dào qiántái kāi jiù xíng.
Yes — after your meal just bring the slip to the front desk.

Why HSK 4 Candidates Struggle with Food & Dining

Describe food flavors, restaurant experiences, and cooking processes. Goes beyond ordering food (HSK 3) to discussing taste, food culture, and sharing dining experiences.

The biggest challenge for HSK 4 candidates on the food & dining topic isn't memorizing the 50 core words — it's understanding how they combine in extended contexts. HSK 4 food & dining questions typically require you to understand cause and effect, opinion shifts, or comparative judgements, not just basic vocabulary recognition.

Key vocabulary to anchor your understanding: 白酒 (báijiǔ, Chinese white liquor), 饼干 (bǐnggān, biscuit), 餐厅 (cāntīng, restaurant), 茶叶 (cháyè, tea leaves). These words frequently appear in HSK 4 listening dialogues (听力) and reading passages (阅读), often with grammar patterns like complement and ba-sentence.

Food & Dining FAQ / 常见问题

How many HSK 4 words cover food & dining?

The HSK 4 official syllabus has 50 core words specifically for the food & dining task scenario. These come from 2 sub-topic categories: food, food-culture. Mastering these 50 words gives you 70-80% comprehension on food & dining-themed questions in HSK 4.

Which HSK 4 grammar points are most relevant to food & dining?

complement and ba-sentence appear most frequently in food & dining contexts. The HSK 4 syllabus expects you to not only understand but also produce these patterns when they involve food & dining vocabulary.

What's the difference between HSK 3 and HSK 4 expectations on food & dining?

HSK 3 expects simple statements about food & dining (e.g., basic facts and short descriptions). HSK 4 raises the bar to "有一定复杂度" — handling extended contexts with opinions, comparisons, and reasoning. You'll need to express feelings (感受) and views (看法) about food & dining, not just describe them.
