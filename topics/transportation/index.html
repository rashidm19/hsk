Scenario Dialogue / 情景对话

Discussing how to get to the airport during rush hour. Read it aloud twice: once for meaning, once for fluency — the syllabus tests this task across listening and speaking.

A
我明天早上八点的飞机，坐出租车去机场来得及吗？
Wǒ míngtiān zǎoshang bā diǎn de fēijī, zuò chūzūchē qù jīchǎng lái de jí ma?
My flight is at eight tomorrow morning — is taking a taxi to the airport fast enough?
B
早高峰路上特别堵，我建议你坐地铁，时间更有保证。
Zǎogāofēng lùshang tèbié dǔ, wǒ jiànyì nǐ zuò dìtiě, shíjiān gèng yǒu bǎozhèng.
Traffic is really congested during morning rush hour. I suggest the subway — the timing is more reliable.
A
地铁要换几次车？我的行李箱挺大的。
Dìtiě yào huàn jǐ cì chē? Wǒ de xínglixiāng tǐng dà de.
How many transfers on the subway? My suitcase is pretty big.
B
只换一次，而且机场线有放行李的地方，很方便。
Zhǐ huàn yí cì, érqiě jīchǎng xiàn yǒu fàng xíngli de dìfang, hěn fāngbiàn.
Just one transfer, and the airport line has luggage racks — very convenient.
A
那我六点出发，应该来得及。谢谢你的建议！
Nà wǒ liù diǎn chūfā, yīnggāi lái de jí. Xièxie nǐ de jiànyì!
Then I will leave at six — that should be enough time. Thanks for the advice!

Why HSK 4 Candidates Struggle with Transportation & Travel

Discuss travel experiences, transportation choices, trip planning, and hotel booking. Includes sharing feelings about journeys and understanding driving/traffic situations.

The biggest challenge for HSK 4 candidates on the transportation & travel topic isn't memorizing the 41 core words — it's understanding how they combine in extended contexts. HSK 4 transportation & travel questions typically require you to understand cause and effect, opinion shifts, or comparative judgements, not just basic vocabulary recognition.

Key vocabulary to anchor your understanding: 背包 (bēibāo, backpack), 车速 (chēsù, vehicle speed), 车位 (chēwèi, parking space), 乘1 (chéng, to ride (a vehicle)). These words frequently appear in HSK 4 listening dialogues (听力) and reading passages (阅读), often with grammar patterns like comparison and complement.

Transportation & Travel FAQ / 常见问题

How many HSK 4 words cover transportation & travel?

The HSK 4 official syllabus has 41 core words specifically for the transportation & travel task scenario. These come from 1 sub-topic categories: transport. Mastering these 41 words gives you 70-80% comprehension on transportation & travel-themed questions in HSK 4.

Which HSK 4 grammar points are most relevant to transportation & travel?

comparison and complement appear most frequently in transportation & travel contexts. The HSK 4 syllabus expects you to not only understand but also produce these patterns when they involve transportation & travel vocabulary.

What's the difference between HSK 3 and HSK 4 expectations on transportation & travel?

HSK 3 expects simple statements about transportation & travel (e.g., basic facts and short descriptions). HSK 4 raises the bar to "有一定复杂度" — handling extended contexts with opinions, comparisons, and reasoning. You'll need to express feelings (感受) and views (看法) about transportation & travel, not just describe them.
