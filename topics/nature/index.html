Scenario Dialogue / 情景对话

Talking about weather differences between north and south. Read it aloud twice: once for meaning, once for fluency — the syllabus tests this task across listening and speaking.

A
你老家在南方，冬天冷吗？
Nǐ lǎojiā zài nánfāng, dōngtiān lěng ma?
Your hometown is in the south — is winter cold there?
B
温度不算低，但是湿度大，感觉比北方还难受。
Wēndù bú suàn dī, dànshì shīdù dà, gǎnjué bǐ běifāng hái nánshòu.
The temperature isn't that low, but it is humid — it actually feels worse than the north.
A
北方虽然零下十几度，可是屋里有暖气，挺舒服的。
Běifāng suīrán língxià shí jǐ dù, kěshì wū li yǒu nuǎnqì, tǐng shūfu de.
In the north it is minus ten-something, but indoors there is heating — quite comfortable.
B
对了，听说你们那儿春天经常刮大风？
Duì le, tīngshuō nǐmen nàr chūntiān jīngcháng guā dàfēng?
By the way, I heard it is often very windy there in spring?
A
是的，有时候还有沙尘。不过秋天特别美，天高云淡。
Shì de, yǒushíhou hái yǒu shāchén. Búguò qiūtiān tèbié měi, tiān gāo yún dàn.
Yes, sometimes with dust. But autumn is gorgeous — clear skies and light clouds.

Why HSK 4 Candidates Struggle with Nature & Geography

Discuss geography, climate, animals, plants, natural landscapes, and weather phenomena. Includes topics like oceans, forests, stars, and seasons.

The biggest challenge for HSK 4 candidates on the nature & geography topic isn't memorizing the 30 core words — it's understanding how they combine in extended contexts. HSK 4 nature & geography questions typically require you to understand cause and effect, opinion shifts, or comparative judgements, not just basic vocabulary recognition.

Key vocabulary to anchor your understanding: 大自然 (dàzìrán, nature), 低温 (dīwēn, low temperature), 风景 (fēngjǐng, scenery), 高温 (gāowēn, high temperature). These words frequently appear in HSK 4 listening dialogues (听力) and reading passages (阅读), often with grammar patterns like complement and comparison.

Nature & Geography FAQ / 常见问题

How many HSK 4 words cover nature & geography?

The HSK 4 official syllabus has 30 core words specifically for the nature & geography task scenario. These come from 1 sub-topic categories: nature. Mastering these 30 words gives you 70-80% comprehension on nature & geography-themed questions in HSK 4.

Which HSK 4 grammar points are most relevant to nature & geography?

complement and comparison appear most frequently in nature & geography contexts. The HSK 4 syllabus expects you to not only understand but also produce these patterns when they involve nature & geography vocabulary.

What's the difference between HSK 3 and HSK 4 expectations on nature & geography?

HSK 3 expects simple statements about nature & geography (e.g., basic facts and short descriptions). HSK 4 raises the bar to "有一定复杂度" — handling extended contexts with opinions, comparisons, and reasoning. You'll need to express feelings (感受) and views (看法) about nature & geography, not just describe them.
