Official Syllabus Requirement / 大纲要求

能听懂关于某种文艺形式、文艺活动、文艺作品创作者及其作品等有一定复杂度的询问。如某部小说、电影、话剧的大致内容、某场文艺表演、某位歌手、作家等。

Scenario Dialogue / 情景对话

Inviting a friend to a concert. Read it aloud twice: once for meaning, once for fluency — the syllabus tests this task across listening and speaking.

A
周五晚上有一场音乐会，我有两张票，一起去吧？
Zhōuwǔ wǎnshang yǒu yì chǎng yīnyuèhuì, wǒ yǒu liǎng zhāng piào, yìqǐ qù ba?
There is a concert Friday evening — I have two tickets. Want to come?
B
太好了！是什么类型的演出？
Tài hǎo le! Shì shénme lèixíng de yǎnchū?
Great! What kind of performance is it?
A
民族音乐，有二胡和古筝，听说演员水平很高。
Mínzú yīnyuè, yǒu èrhú hé gǔzhēng, tīngshuō yǎnyuán shuǐpíng hěn gāo.
Traditional Chinese music with erhu and guzheng — the performers are supposed to be excellent.
B
我一直想现场听一次古筝！演出几点开始？
Wǒ yìzhí xiǎng xiànchǎng tīng yí cì gǔzhēng! Yǎnchū jǐ diǎn kāishǐ?
I have always wanted to hear the guzheng live! What time does it start?
A
七点半。我们提前一点儿到，演出开始后就不让进了。
Qī diǎn bàn. Wǒmen tíqián yìdiǎnr dào, yǎnchū kāishǐ hòu jiù bú ràng jìn le.
7:30. Let's arrive early — no entry after the performance begins.

Arts & Entertainment FAQ / 常见问题

How many HSK 4 words cover arts & entertainment?

The HSK 4 official syllabus has 19 core words specifically for the arts & entertainment task scenario. These come from 1 sub-topic categories: arts. Mastering these 19 words gives you 70-80% comprehension on arts & entertainment-themed questions in HSK 4.

Which HSK 4 grammar points are most relevant to arts & entertainment?

complement and fixed-patterns appear most frequently in arts & entertainment contexts. The HSK 4 syllabus expects you to not only understand but also produce these patterns when they involve arts & entertainment vocabulary.

What's the difference between HSK 3 and HSK 4 expectations on arts & entertainment?

HSK 3 expects simple statements about arts & entertainment (e.g., basic facts and short descriptions). HSK 4 raises the bar to "有一定复杂度" — handling extended contexts with opinions, comparisons, and reasoning. You'll need to express feelings (感受) and views (看法) about arts & entertainment, not just describe them.
