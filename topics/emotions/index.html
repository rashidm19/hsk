Official Syllabus Requirement / 大纲要求

Scenario Dialogue / 情景对话

Two friends talk about friendship and ideals. Read it aloud twice: once for meaning, once for fluency — the syllabus tests this task across listening and speaking.

A
我们认识十年了，真正的友谊经得起时间的考验。
Wǒmen rènshi shí nián le, zhēnzhèng de yǒuyì jīng de qǐ shíjiān de kǎoyàn.
We have known each other for ten years — true friendship stands the test of time.
B
是啊，遇到困难的时候，第一个想到的就是你。
Shì a, yùdào kùnnan de shíhou, dì yī gè xiǎngdào de jiùshì nǐ.
Yes — whenever I run into difficulties, you are the first person I think of.
A
对了，你小时候的理想是什么？
Duì le, nǐ xiǎoshíhou de lǐxiǎng shì shénme?
By the way, what was your dream when you were little?
B
我一直想当医生，帮助更多的人。虽然过程很辛苦，但我从来没放弃过。
Wǒ yìzhí xiǎng dāng yīshēng, bāngzhù gèng duō de rén. Suīrán guòchéng hěn xīnkǔ, dàn wǒ cónglái méi fàngqì guo.
I always wanted to be a doctor and help more people. The process is hard, but I have never given up.
A
有理想就要坚持，我相信你一定能实现。
Yǒu lǐxiǎng jiù yào jiānchí, wǒ xiāngxìn nǐ yídìng néng shíxiàn.
If you have a dream, stick to it — I believe you will make it come true.

Why HSK 4 Candidates Struggle with Discuss Emotions

Discuss love, friendship, family bonds, and ideals. HSK 4 requires not just naming emotions but sharing experiences and opinions about them — “What does friendship mean to you?” rather than “I am happy.”

The biggest challenge for HSK 4 candidates on the discuss emotions topic isn't memorizing the 82 core words — it's understanding how they combine in extended contexts. HSK 4 discuss emotions questions typically require you to understand cause and effect, opinion shifts, or comparative judgements, not just basic vocabulary recognition.

Key vocabulary to anchor your understanding: 爱情 (àiqíng, romantic love), 爱心 (àixīn, love), 抱 (bào, to hug), 抱歉 (bàoqiàn, sorry). These words frequently appear in HSK 4 listening dialogues (听力) and reading passages (阅读), often with grammar patterns like complex-sentences and adverbs.
