Official Syllabus Requirement / 大纲要求

能听懂对方讲述的有一定复杂度的中外友好往来的故事及其产生的影响。如友好城市、友好学校、跨国友谊、留学经历、中文比赛经历等。

Scenario Dialogue / 情景对话

An exchange student talks about studying in China. Read it aloud twice: once for meaning, once for fluency — the syllabus tests this task across listening and speaking.

A
你来中国留学两年了，最大的收获是什么？
Nǐ lái Zhōngguó liúxué liǎng nián le, zuì dà de shōuhuò shì shénme?
You have studied in China for two years — what is your biggest gain?
B
除了汉语，还交了很多中国朋友。去年春节我是在同学家过的。
Chúle Hànyǔ, hái jiāo le hěn duō Zhōngguó péngyou. Qùnián Chūnjié wǒ shì zài tóngxué jiā guò de.
Besides Chinese, I made many Chinese friends. Last Spring Festival I celebrated at a classmate's home.
A
真好！你们国家和中国的交流多吗？
Zhēn hǎo! Nǐmen guójiā hé Zhōngguó de jiāoliú duō ma?
How nice! Is there much exchange between your country and China?
B
越来越多了，两国大学每年都互相派留学生，还有文化节。
Yuè lái yuè duō le, liǎng guó dàxué měi nián dōu hùxiāng pài liúxuéshēng, hái yǒu wénhuàjié.
More and more — our universities exchange students every year, and there are culture festivals.
A
交流让两国人民的友谊越来越深。
Jiāoliú ràng liǎng guó rénmín de yǒuyì yuè lái yuè shēn.
Exchange keeps deepening the friendship between our two peoples.

Why HSK 4 Candidates Struggle with China-World Friendship

The biggest challenge for HSK 4 candidates on the china-world friendship topic isn't memorizing the 21 core words — it's understanding how they combine in extended contexts. HSK 4 china-world friendship questions typically require you to understand cause and effect, opinion shifts, or comparative judgements, not just basic vocabulary recognition.

Key vocabulary to anchor your understanding: 参赛 (cānsài, to compete), 大赛 (dàsài, large competition), 翻译 (fānyì, to translate), 国籍 (guójí, nationality). These words frequently appear in HSK 4 listening dialogues (听力) and reading passages (阅读), often with grammar patterns like complex-sentences and fixed-patterns.

China-World Friendship FAQ / 常见问题

How many HSK 4 words cover china-world friendship?

The HSK 4 official syllabus has 21 core words specifically for the china-world friendship task scenario. These come from 1 sub-topic categories: exchange. Mastering these 21 words gives you 70-80% comprehension on china-world friendship-themed questions in HSK 4.

Which HSK 4 grammar points are most relevant to china-world friendship?

complex-sentences and fixed-patterns appear most frequently in china-world friendship contexts. The HSK 4 syllabus expects you to not only understand but also produce these patterns when they involve china-world friendship vocabulary.

What's the difference between HSK 3 and HSK 4 expectations on china-world friendship?

HSK 3 expects simple statements about china-world friendship (e.g., basic facts and short descriptions). HSK 4 raises the bar to "有一定复杂度" — handling extended contexts with opinions, comparisons, and reasoning. You'll need to express feelings (感受) and views (看法) about china-world friendship, not just describe them.
