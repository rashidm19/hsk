Official Syllabus Requirement / 大纲要求

能听懂朋友、同学、老师等对中国某个名胜古迹的一般性介绍。如天安门、长城等。能看懂介绍中国某个名胜古迹的一般性短文；能写出一段话简单介绍中国某个名胜古迹。

Scenario Dialogue / 情景对话

Planning a visit to the Great Wall. Read it aloud twice: once for meaning, once for fluency — the syllabus tests this task across listening and speaking.

A
这周六我们去爬长城吧！都说"不到长城非好汉"。
Zhè zhōuliù wǒmen qù pá Chángchéng ba! Dōu shuō "bú dào Chángchéng fēi hǎohàn".
Let's climb the Great Wall this Saturday! As they say, "You're not a true hero until you've been to the Great Wall."
B
好啊！去八达岭还是慕田峪？
Hǎo a! Qù Bādálǐng háishi Mùtiányù?
Sure! Badaling or Mutianyu?
A
慕田峪人少一些，风景也美，就是远一点儿。
Mùtiányù rén shǎo yìxiē, fēngjǐng yě měi, jiùshì yuǎn yìdiǎnr.
Mutianyu is less crowded and beautiful — just a bit farther.
B
没关系，我们早点儿出发。门票要提前在网上订吗？
Méi guānxi, wǒmen zǎodiǎnr chūfā. Ménpiào yào tíqián zài wǎngshàng dìng ma?
No problem, we will leave early. Should we book tickets online in advance?
A
要，周末游客多，提前订还能便宜十块钱。
Yào, zhōumò yóukè duō, tíqián dìng hái néng piányi shí kuài qián.
Yes — weekends are busy, and booking ahead saves ten yuan.
B
那就这么定了，我顺便查查天气预报。
Nà jiù zhème dìng le, wǒ shùnbiàn chácha tiānqì yùbào.
Settled then — I will check the weather forecast while I am at it.

What's the difference between HSK 3 and HSK 4 expectations on scenic spots & historic sites?

HSK 3 expects simple statements about scenic spots & historic sites (e.g., basic facts and short descriptions). You'll need to express feelings (感受) and views (看法) about scenic spots & historic sites, not just describe them.
