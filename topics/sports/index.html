Scenario Dialogue / 情景对话

Talking about a basketball match and exercise habits. Read it aloud twice: once for meaning, once for fluency — the syllabus tests this task across listening and speaking.

A
昨天晚上的篮球比赛你看了吗？
Zuótiān wǎnshang de lánqiú bǐsài nǐ kàn le ma?
Did you watch the basketball game last night?
B
看了！最后三秒才决定胜负，太精彩了！
Kàn le! Zuìhòu sān miǎo cái juédìng shèngfù, tài jīngcǎi le!
I did! It was decided in the final three seconds — so exciting!
A
你平时也打篮球吗？
Nǐ píngshí yě dǎ lánqiú ma?
Do you play basketball yourself?
B
每周打两次，再加上跑步。坚持锻炼以后，感冒都少了。
Měi zhōu dǎ liǎng cì, zài jiāshang pǎobù. Jiānchí duànliàn yǐhòu, gǎnmào dōu shǎo le.
Twice a week, plus running. Since I started exercising regularly I rarely catch colds.
A
我也得动起来，要不周末跟你一起打？
Wǒ yě děi dòng qǐlái, yàobù zhōumò gēn nǐ yìqǐ dǎ?
I should get moving too — how about I join you this weekend?
B
欢迎欢迎，运动贵在坚持！
Huānyíng huānyíng, yùndòng guì zài jiānchí!
Welcome! With sports, consistency is what counts!

Why HSK 4 Candidates Struggle with Sports

Discuss sports like table tennis, volleyball, and badminton; competition results, player performances, and sports stories.

The biggest challenge for HSK 4 candidates on the sports topic isn't memorizing the 14 core words — it's understanding how they combine in extended contexts. HSK 4 sports questions typically require you to understand cause and effect, opinion shifts, or comparative judgements, not just basic vocabulary recognition.

Key vocabulary to anchor your understanding: 打败 (dǎbài, to defeat), 动作 (dòngzuò, action), 功夫 (gōngfu, kung fu), 健身 (jiànshēn, to work out). These words frequently appear in HSK 4 listening dialogues (听力) and reading passages (阅读), often with grammar patterns like comparison and complement.

Sports FAQ / 常见问题

How many HSK 4 words cover sports?

The HSK 4 official syllabus has 14 core words specifically for the sports task scenario. These come from 1 sub-topic categories: sports. Mastering these 14 words gives you 70-80% comprehension on sports-themed questions in HSK 4.

Which HSK 4 grammar points are most relevant to sports?

comparison and complement appear most frequently in sports contexts. The HSK 4 syllabus expects you to not only understand but also produce these patterns when they involve sports vocabulary.

What's the difference between HSK 3 and HSK 4 expectations on sports?

HSK 3 expects simple statements about sports (e.g., basic facts and short descriptions). HSK 4 raises the bar to "有一定复杂度" — handling extended contexts with opinions, comparisons, and reasoning. You'll need to express feelings (感受) and views (看法) about sports, not just describe them.
