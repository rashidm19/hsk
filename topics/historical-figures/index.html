Official Syllabus Requirement / 大纲要求

Scenario Dialogue / 情景对话

After visiting a museum, two friends talk about Confucius. Read it aloud twice: once for meaning, once for fluency — the syllabus tests this task across listening and speaking.

A
今天博物馆里介绍的孔子，到底是个什么样的人？
Jīntiān bówùguǎn li jièshào de Kǒngzǐ, dàodǐ shì ge shénmeyàng de rén?
The museum today introduced Confucius — what kind of person was he exactly?
B
他是两千多年前的教育家和思想家，最早提出"因材施教"。
Tā shì liǎngqiān duō nián qián de jiàoyùjiā hé sīxiǎngjiā, zuì zǎo tíchū "yīn cái shī jiào".
He was an educator and thinker from over two thousand years ago — the first to propose teaching students according to their aptitude.
A
难怪有那么多人尊重他。他的学生很多吗？
Nánguài yǒu nàme duō rén zūnzhòng tā. Tā de xuésheng hěn duō ma?
No wonder so many people respect him. Did he have many students?
B
传说有三千弟子。他的思想记录在《论语》里，到今天还影响着中国人。
Chuánshuō yǒu sānqiān dìzǐ. Tā de sīxiǎng jìlù zài "Lúnyǔ" li, dào jīntiān hái yǐngxiǎng zhe Zhōngguórén.
Three thousand, legend says. His ideas are recorded in the Analects and still influence Chinese people today.
A
"三人行，必有我师"就是他说的吧？我特别喜欢这句话。
"Sān rén xíng, bì yǒu wǒ shī" jiùshì tā shuō de ba? Wǒ tèbié xǐhuan zhè jù huà.
"Among any three people walking, one can be my teacher" — that was him, right? I love that line.

Historical Figures & Events FAQ / 常见问题

How many HSK 4 words cover historical figures & events?

The HSK 4 official syllabus has 21 core words specifically for the historical figures & events task scenario. These come from 1 sub-topic categories: history. Mastering these 21 words gives you 70-80% comprehension on historical figures & events-themed questions in HSK 4.

Which HSK 4 grammar points are most relevant to historical figures & events?

complex-sentences and function-words appear most frequently in historical figures & events contexts. The HSK 4 syllabus expects you to understand these patterns when they involve historical figures & events vocabulary.

What's the difference between HSK 3 and HSK 4 expectations on historical figures & events?

HSK 3 expects simple statements about historical figures & events (e.g., basic facts and short descriptions). HSK 4 raises the bar to "有一定复杂度" — handling extended contexts with opinions, comparisons, and reasoning. You'll need to express feelings (感受) and views (看法) about historical figures & events, not just describe them.
