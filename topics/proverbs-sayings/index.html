Scenario Dialogue / 情景对话

A student asks about a common Chinese saying. Read it aloud twice: once for meaning, once for fluency — the syllabus tests this task across listening and speaking.

A
老师常说"熟能生巧"，这是什么意思？
Lǎoshī cháng shuō "shú néng shēng qiǎo", zhè shì shénme yìsi?
The teacher often says "shú néng shēng qiǎo" — what does it mean?
B
意思是做的次数多了，自然就掌握了技巧，跟英语的 practice makes perfect 一样。
Yìsi shì zuò de cìshù duō le, zìrán jiù zhǎngwò le jìqiǎo, gēn Yīngyǔ de practice makes perfect yíyàng.
It means doing something many times naturally makes you skilled — like "practice makes perfect" in English.
A
原来如此！那"万事开头难"呢？
Yuánlái rúcǐ! Nà "wànshì kāitóu nán" ne?
I see! And what about "wànshì kāitóu nán"?
B
就是说任何事情开始的时候最难，坚持下去就会容易起来。
Jiùshì shuō rènhé shìqing kāishǐ de shíhou zuì nán, jiānchí xiàqù jiù huì róngyì qǐlái.
It means the beginning of anything is the hardest part — keep going and it gets easier.
A
汉语的俗语真有意思，一句话就能讲清楚一个道理。
Hànyǔ de súyǔ zhēn yǒu yìsi, yí jù huà jiù néng jiǎng qīngchu yí gè dàolǐ.
Chinese sayings are fascinating — one sentence can capture a whole truth.

Why HSK 4 Candidates Struggle with Proverbs & Sayings

Understand and roughly explain common Chinese sayings and famous quotes. The syllabus tests this receptively — recognizing what a saying means when you hear it in conversation or meet it in a short reading passage.

The biggest challenge for HSK 4 candidates on the proverbs & sayings topic isn't memorizing the 21 core words — it's understanding how they combine in extended contexts. HSK 4 proverbs & sayings questions typically require you to understand cause and effect, opinion shifts, or comparative judgements, not just basic vocabulary recognition.

Key vocabulary to anchor your understanding: 表示 (biǎoshì, to express), 词语 (cíyǔ, word), 翻译 (fānyì, to translate), 解释 (jiěshì, to explain). These words frequently appear in HSK 4 listening dialogues (听力) and reading passages (阅读), often with grammar patterns like fixed-patterns and rhetorical.

Proverbs & Sayings FAQ / 常见问题

How many HSK 4 words cover proverbs & sayings?

The HSK 4 official syllabus has 21 core words specifically for the proverbs & sayings task scenario. These come from 1 sub-topic categories: language. Mastering these 21 words gives you 70-80% comprehension on proverbs & sayings-themed questions in HSK 4.

Which HSK 4 grammar points are most relevant to proverbs & sayings?

fixed-patterns and rhetorical appear most frequently in proverbs & sayings contexts. The HSK 4 syllabus expects you to understand these patterns when they involve proverbs & sayings vocabulary.

What's the difference between HSK 3 and HSK 4 expectations on proverbs & sayings?

HSK 3 expects simple statements about proverbs & sayings (e.g., basic facts and short descriptions). HSK 4 raises the bar to "有一定复杂度" — handling extended contexts with opinions, comparisons, and reasoning. You'll need to express feelings (感受) and views (看法) about proverbs & sayings, not just describe them.
