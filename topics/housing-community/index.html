Official Syllabus Requirement / 大纲要求

能听懂关于居住情况、社区生活、房屋租赁与买卖等情况的有一定复杂度的询问。如小区环境、邻里相处、租房条件等。

Scenario Dialogue / 情景对话

Asking a real estate agent about renting an apartment. Read it aloud twice: once for meaning, once for fluency — the syllabus tests this task across listening and speaking.

A
您好，我想租一套两室一厅的房子，最好离地铁站近一点儿。
Nín hǎo, wǒ xiǎng zū yí tào liǎng shì yì tīng de fángzi, zuìhǎo lí dìtiězhàn jìn yìdiǎnr.
Hello, I am looking to rent a two-bedroom apartment, ideally close to a subway station.
B
有一套正合适，走到地铁站只要五分钟，家具家电都是全的。
Yǒu yí tào zhèng héshì, zǒu dào dìtiězhàn zhǐ yào wǔ fēnzhōng, jiājù jiādiàn dōu shì quán de.
There is one that fits perfectly — five minutes' walk to the subway, fully furnished with appliances.
A
房租多少？小区环境怎么样？
Fángzū duōshao? Xiǎoqū huánjìng zěnmeyàng?
What is the rent? How is the neighborhood?
B
一个月四千五。小区很安静，楼下就有超市，对面还有一个公园。
Yí gè yuè sìqiān wǔ. Xiǎoqū hěn ānjìng, lóu xià jiù yǒu chāoshì, duìmiàn hái yǒu yí gè gōngyuán.
4,500 a month. The community is quiet, there is a supermarket downstairs and a park across the street.
A
听起来不错，今天下午可以看房吗？
Tīng qǐlái búcuò, jīntiān xiàwǔ kěyǐ kàn fáng ma?
Sounds good — can I view it this afternoon?
B
可以，三点我在小区门口等您。
Kěyǐ, sān diǎn wǒ zài xiǎoqū ménkǒu děng nín.
Sure, I will wait for you at the community gate at three.

Why HSK 4 Candidates Struggle with Housing & Community

The biggest challenge for HSK 4 candidates on the housing & community topic isn't memorizing the 29 core words — it's understanding how they combine in extended contexts. HSK 4 housing & community questions typically require you to understand cause and effect, opinion shifts, or comparative judgements, not just basic vocabulary recognition.

Key vocabulary to anchor your understanding: 厕所 (cèsuǒ, toilet), 出租 (chūzū, to rent out), 厨房 (chúfáng, kitchen), 窗 (chuāng, window). These words frequently appear in HSK 4 listening dialogues (听力) and reading passages (阅读), often with grammar patterns like comparison and passive.

Housing & Community FAQ / 常见问题

How many HSK 4 words cover housing & community?

The HSK 4 official syllabus has 29 core words specifically for the housing & community task scenario. These come from 1 sub-topic categories: community. Mastering these 29 words gives you 70-80% comprehension on housing & community-themed questions in HSK 4.

Which HSK 4 grammar points are most relevant to housing & community?

comparison and passive appear most frequently in housing & community contexts. The HSK 4 syllabus expects you to not only understand but also produce these patterns when they involve housing & community vocabulary.

What's the difference between HSK 3 and HSK 4 expectations on housing & community?

HSK 3 expects simple statements about housing & community (e.g., basic facts and short descriptions). HSK 4 raises the bar to "有一定复杂度" — handling extended contexts with opinions, comparisons, and reasoning. You'll need to express feelings (感受) and views (看法) about housing & community, not just describe them.
