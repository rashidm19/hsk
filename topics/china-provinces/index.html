Scenario Dialogue / 情景对话

Planning which Chinese cities to visit. Read it aloud twice: once for meaning, once for fluency — the syllabus tests this task across listening and speaking.

A
我放假想在中国旅行，你推荐去哪儿？
Wǒ fàngjià xiǎng zài Zhōngguó lǚxíng, nǐ tuījiàn qù nǎr?
I want to travel in China during the holiday — where do you recommend?
B
看你喜欢什么。喜欢历史就去北京、西安，喜欢现代城市就去上海、深圳。
Kàn nǐ xǐhuan shénme. Xǐhuan lìshǐ jiù qù Běijīng, Xī'ān, xǐhuan xiàndài chéngshì jiù qù Shànghǎi, Shēnzhèn.
Depends what you like. For history go to Beijing or Xi'an; for modern cities, Shanghai or Shenzhen.
A
听说云南的少数民族文化很有特色？
Tīngshuō Yúnnán de shǎoshù mínzú wénhuà hěn yǒu tèsè?
I heard Yunnan's ethnic minority cultures are very distinctive?
B
对，云南有二十多个民族，节日、服装、饮食都不一样，风景也美。
Duì, Yúnnán yǒu èrshí duō gè mínzú, jiérì, fúzhuāng, yǐnshí dōu bù yíyàng, fēngjǐng yě měi.
Yes — Yunnan has over twenty ethnic groups with different festivals, clothing and food, and beautiful scenery.
A
那就先去云南！再顺便去四川看大熊猫。
Nà jiù xiān qù Yúnnán! Zài shùnbiàn qù Sìchuān kàn dàxióngmāo.
Yunnan first then! And Sichuan on the way to see the pandas.

What's the difference between HSK 3 and HSK 4 expectations on china overview?
