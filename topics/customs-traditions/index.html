Official Syllabus Requirement / 大纲要求

能听懂朋友、同学、老师等对中国传统节日习俗、国粹、各地传统、人际交往礼仪等中国民俗传统相关情况的有一定复杂度的介绍。如春节、中秋节等节日习俗；中国功夫、京剧等国粹；民间喜好与禁忌等。

Scenario Dialogue / 情景对话

Talking about Mid-Autumn Festival customs. Read it aloud twice: once for meaning, once for fluency — the syllabus tests this task across listening and speaking.

A
快到中秋节了，你们家有什么习俗？
Kuài dào Zhōngqiūjié le, nǐmen jiā yǒu shénme xísú?
Mid-Autumn Festival is coming — what customs does your family have?
B
全家一起吃月饼、赏月。中秋讲究的就是团圆。
Quánjiā yìqǐ chī yuèbǐng, shǎng yuè. Zhōngqiū jiǎngjiu de jiùshì tuányuán.
The whole family eats mooncakes and admires the moon together. Mid-Autumn is all about reunion.
A
要是家人在外地回不来呢？
Yàoshi jiārén zài wàidì huí bu lái ne?
What if family members are away and can't come back?
B
那就打视频电话，"千里共婵娟"嘛，看的是同一个月亮。
Nà jiù dǎ shìpín diànhuà, "qiānlǐ gòng chánjuān" ma, kàn de shì tóng yí gè yuèliang.
Then we video call — as the poem says, though a thousand miles apart, we share the same moon.
A
真浪漫。送月饼有什么讲究吗？
Zhēn làngmàn. Sòng yuèbǐng yǒu shénme jiǎngjiu ma?
How romantic. Are there customs about giving mooncakes?
B
给长辈送双数的，表示祝福。下周我带你去尝尝我家乡的鲜肉月饼。
Gěi zhǎngbèi sòng shuāngshù de, biǎoshì zhùfú. Xià zhōu wǒ dài nǐ qù chángchang wǒ jiāxiāng de xiānròu yuèbǐng.
Give elders an even number, as a blessing. Next week I will take you to try my hometown's fresh-meat mooncakes.

Why HSK 4 Candidates Struggle with Customs & Traditions

Understand and introduce Chinese folk traditions: festival customs like Spring Festival and Mid-Autumn, national arts like kung fu and Peking opera, regional traditions, and the etiquette of interacting with friends, teachers and elders.

The biggest challenge for HSK 4 candidates on the customs & traditions topic isn't memorizing the 18 core words — it's understanding how they combine in extended contexts. HSK 4 customs & traditions questions typically require you to understand cause and effect, opinion shifts, or comparative judgements, not just basic vocabulary recognition.

Key vocabulary to anchor your understanding: 打招呼 (dǎ zhāohu, to greet), 各地 (gèdì, various places), 功夫 (gōngfu, kung fu), 红包 (hóngbāo, red envelope (gift money)). These words frequently appear in HSK 4 listening dialogues (听力) and reading passages (阅读), often with grammar patterns like fixed-patterns and function-words.

Customs & Traditions FAQ / 常见问题

How many HSK 4 words cover customs & traditions?

The HSK 4 official syllabus has 18 core words specifically for the customs & traditions task scenario. These come from 2 sub-topic categories: customs, etiquette. Mastering these 18 words gives you 70-80% comprehension on customs & traditions-themed questions in HSK 4.

Which HSK 4 grammar points are most relevant to customs & traditions?

fixed-patterns and function-words appear most frequently in customs & traditions contexts. The HSK 4 syllabus expects you to not only understand but also produce these patterns when they involve customs & traditions vocabulary.

What's the difference between HSK 3 and HSK 4 expectations on customs & traditions?

HSK 3 expects simple statements about customs & traditions (e.g., basic facts and short descriptions). HSK 4 raises the bar to "有一定复杂度" — handling extended contexts with opinions, comparisons, and reasoning. You'll need to express feelings (感受) and views (看法) about customs & traditions, not just describe them.
