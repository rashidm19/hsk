Official Syllabus Requirement / 大纲要求

能听懂关于环境状况、环保情况有一定复杂度的问题。如环境的一般情况、污染情况、环保做法、观念、相关法规等。

Scenario Dialogue / 情景对话

Roommates discuss garbage sorting and saving energy. Read it aloud twice: once for meaning, once for fluency — the syllabus tests this task across listening and speaking.

A
咱们小区开始垃圾分类了，你知道怎么分吗？
Zánmen xiǎoqū kāishǐ lājī fēnlèi le, nǐ zhīdào zěnme fēn ma?
Our community has started garbage sorting — do you know how to sort?
B
厨房的剩菜是厨余垃圾，塑料瓶和纸盒可以回收。
Chúfáng de shèngcài shì chúyú lājī, sùliào píng hé zhǐhé kěyǐ huíshōu.
Kitchen leftovers are food waste; plastic bottles and paper boxes are recyclable.
A
明白了。其实保护环境还可以从小事做起，比如随手关灯。
Míngbai le. Qíshí bǎohù huánjìng hái kěyǐ cóng xiǎoshì zuò qǐ, bǐrú suíshǒu guān dēng.
Got it. Actually protecting the environment starts with small things — like turning off lights.
B
对，我现在出门都自己带购物袋，少用塑料袋。
Duì, wǒ xiànzài chūmén dōu zìjǐ dài gòuwùdài, shǎo yòng sùliàodài.
Right — I bring my own shopping bag now and use fewer plastic ones.
A
要是每个人都这样做，环境一定会越来越好。
Yàoshi měi gè rén dōu zhèyàng zuò, huánjìng yídìng huì yuè lái yuè hǎo.
If everyone did this, the environment would definitely get better and better.

Why HSK 4 Candidates Struggle with Environmental Protection

Discuss environmental conditions, pollution, conservation practices, environmental laws, and green living.

The biggest challenge for HSK 4 candidates on the environmental protection topic isn't memorizing the 38 core words — it's understanding how they combine in extended contexts. HSK 4 environmental protection questions typically require you to understand cause and effect, opinion shifts, or comparative judgements, not just basic vocabulary recognition.

Key vocabulary to anchor your understanding: 保护 (bǎohù, to protect), 环保 (huánbǎo, protection), 节约 (jiéyuē, to save), 空气 (kōngqì, air). These words frequently appear in HSK 4 listening dialogues (听力) and reading passages (阅读), often with grammar patterns like complex-sentences and passive.

Environmental Protection FAQ / 常见问题

How many HSK 4 words cover environmental protection?

The HSK 4 official syllabus has 38 core words specifically for the environmental protection task scenario. These come from 2 sub-topic categories: environment, nature. Mastering these 38 words gives you 70-80% comprehension on environmental protection-themed questions in HSK 4.

Which HSK 4 grammar points are most relevant to environmental protection?

complex-sentences and passive appear most frequently in environmental protection contexts. The HSK 4 syllabus expects you to not only understand but also produce these patterns when they involve environmental protection vocabulary.

What's the difference between HSK 3 and HSK 4 expectations on environmental protection?

HSK 3 expects simple statements about environmental protection (e.g., basic facts and short descriptions). HSK 4 raises the bar to "有一定复杂度" — handling extended contexts with opinions, comparisons, and reasoning. You'll need to express feelings (感受) and views (看法) about environmental protection, not just describe them.
