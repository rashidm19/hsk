Official Syllabus Requirement / 大纲要求

能听懂他人关于某个熟人或公众人物个人信息、个人特征方面有一定复杂度的问题。如履历、家庭背景、职业背景、外貌、装扮、性格、影响力等。

Scenario Dialogue / 情景对话

Two classmates talk about their new Chinese teacher. Read it aloud twice: once for meaning, once for fluency — the syllabus tests this task across listening and speaking.

A
你觉得新来的王老师怎么样？
Nǐ juéde xīn lái de Wáng lǎoshī zěnmeyàng?
What do you think of our new teacher, Ms. Wang?
B
她很有经验，对学生也特别耐心。
Tā hěn yǒu jīngyàn, duì xuésheng yě tèbié nàixīn.
She is very experienced and especially patient with students.
A
听说她在国外教过五年中文，难怪发音讲得那么清楚。
Tīngshuō tā zài guówài jiāo guo wǔ nián Zhōngwén, nánguài fāyīn jiǎng de nàme qīngchu.
I heard she taught Chinese abroad for five years — no wonder she explains pronunciation so clearly.
B
对，她性格幽默，上课的时候大家都很活跃。
Duì, tā xìnggé yōumò, shàngkè de shíhou dàjiā dōu hěn huóyuè.
Right, she has a humorous personality; everyone is very active in her class.
A
有这样的老师，我们的汉语水平一定会提高得很快。
Yǒu zhèyàng de lǎoshī, wǒmen de Hànyǔ shuǐpíng yídìng huì tígāo de hěn kuài.
With a teacher like her, our Chinese will definitely improve quickly.

Why HSK 4 Candidates Struggle with Describe a Person

Discuss someone’s background, appearance, personality, and influence. The syllabus requires handling “有一定复杂度” (a certain complexity) — not just “he is tall” but describing someone’s career background, character traits, and impact.

The biggest challenge for HSK 4 candidates on the describe a person topic isn't memorizing the 96 core words — it's understanding how they combine in extended contexts. HSK 4 describe a person questions typically require you to understand cause and effect, opinion shifts, or comparative judgements, not just basic vocabulary recognition.

Key vocabulary to anchor your understanding: 笨 (bèn, stupid), 鼻子 (bízi, nose), 诚实 (chéngshí, honest), 粗心 (cūxīn, careless). These words frequently appear in HSK 4 listening dialogues (听力) and reading passages (阅读), often with grammar patterns like ba-sentence and complement.

How many HSK 4 words cover describe a person?

The HSK 4 official syllabus has 96 core words specifically for the describe a person task scenario. These come from 2 sub-topic categories: personal, social. Mastering these 96 words gives you 70-80% comprehension on describe a person-themed questions in HSK 4.

Which HSK 4 grammar points are most relevant to describe a person?

ba-sentence and complement appear most frequently in describe a person contexts. The HSK 4 syllabus expects you to not only understand but also produce these patterns when they involve describe a person vocabulary.

What's the difference between HSK 3 and HSK 4 expectations on describe a person?

HSK 3 expects simple statements about describe a person (e.g., basic facts and short descriptions). HSK 4 raises the bar to "有一定复杂度" — handling extended contexts with opinions, comparisons, and reasoning. You'll need to express feelings (感受) and views (看法) about describe a person, not just describe them.
