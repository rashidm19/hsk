Scenario Dialogue / 情景对话

Comparing online and in-store purchases with a friend. Read it aloud twice: once for meaning, once for fluency — the syllabus tests this task across listening and speaking.

A
你这双鞋真好看，在哪儿买的？
Nǐ zhè shuāng xié zhēn hǎokàn, zài nǎr mǎi de?
Your shoes look great — where did you buy them?
B
网上买的，正好赶上打折，比商场便宜两百多块。
Wǎngshàng mǎi de, zhènghǎo gǎnshàng dǎzhé, bǐ shāngchǎng piányi liǎngbǎi duō kuài.
Online — there happened to be a sale, over two hundred yuan cheaper than the mall.
A
网购是便宜，可是我总担心质量没有保证。
Wǎnggòu shì piányi, kěshì wǒ zǒng dānxīn zhìliàng méiyǒu bǎozhèng.
Online shopping is cheap, but I always worry the quality isn't guaranteed.
B
选信用好的店就行。不合适还可以七天无理由退货，挺方便的。
Xuǎn xìnyòng hǎo de diàn jiù xíng. Bù héshì hái kěyǐ qī tiān wú lǐyóu tuìhuò, tǐng fāngbiàn de.
Just pick shops with good ratings. If it doesn't fit you can return it within seven days, no questions asked — quite convenient.
A
那你把店铺链接发给我，我也去看看。
Nà nǐ bǎ diànpù liànjiē fā gěi wǒ, wǒ yě qù kànkan.
Then send me the shop's link — I'll take a look too.

Why HSK 4 Candidates Struggle with Shopping Experiences

Discuss product selection, online shopping, brand choices, spending, payment methods, and sales promotions. HSK 4 goes beyond price negotiation to evaluating shopping experiences.

The biggest challenge for HSK 4 candidates on the shopping experiences topic isn't memorizing the 30 core words — it's understanding how they combine in extended contexts. HSK 4 shopping experiences questions typically require you to understand cause and effect, opinion shifts, or comparative judgements, not just basic vocabulary recognition.

Key vocabulary to anchor your understanding: 存 (cún, to save), 打折 (dǎzhé, to discount), 低价 (dījià, low price), 订 (dìng, to book). These words frequently appear in HSK 4 listening dialogues (听力) and reading passages (阅读), often with grammar patterns like comparison and adverbs.

Shopping Experiences FAQ / 常见问题

How many HSK 4 words cover shopping experiences?

The HSK 4 official syllabus has 30 core words specifically for the shopping experiences task scenario. These come from 1 sub-topic categories: shopping. Mastering these 30 words gives you 70-80% comprehension on shopping experiences-themed questions in HSK 4.

Which HSK 4 grammar points are most relevant to shopping experiences?

comparison and adverbs appear most frequently in shopping experiences contexts. The HSK 4 syllabus expects you to not only understand but also produce these patterns when they involve shopping experiences vocabulary.

What's the difference between HSK 3 and HSK 4 expectations on shopping experiences?

HSK 3 expects simple statements about shopping experiences (e.g., basic facts and short descriptions). HSK 4 raises the bar to "有一定复杂度" — handling extended contexts with opinions, comparisons, and reasoning. You'll need to express feelings (感受) and views (看法) about shopping experiences, not just describe them.
