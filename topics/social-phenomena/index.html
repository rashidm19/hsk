Scenario Dialogue / 情景对话

Discussing how phones change daily life. Read it aloud twice: once for meaning, once for fluency — the syllabus tests this task across listening and speaking.

A
你发现没有，现在地铁里几乎人人都在看手机。
Nǐ fāxiàn méiyǒu, xiànzài dìtiě li jīhū rénrén dōu zài kàn shǒujī.
Have you noticed? On the subway now almost everyone is looking at their phone.
B
是啊，网络改变了我们的生活方式，连点菜、挂号都在手机上。
Shì a, wǎngluò gǎibiàn le wǒmen de shēnghuó fāngshì, lián diǎn cài, guàhào dōu zài shǒujī shang.
Yes — the internet has changed how we live; even ordering food and booking doctor visits happen on the phone.
A
方便是方便，不过人和人之间的交流好像变少了。
Fāngbiàn shì fāngbiàn, búguò rén hé rén zhījiān de jiāoliú hǎoxiàng biàn shǎo le.
Convenient, sure — but face-to-face communication seems to have decreased.
B
所以我们家规定，吃饭的时候谁都不许玩儿手机。
Suǒyǐ wǒmen jiā guīdìng, chīfàn de shíhou shéi dōu bù xǔ wánr shǒujī.
That is why my family has a rule: no phones at the dinner table.
A
这个办法不错，科技应该让生活更好，而不是让人更远。
Zhège bànfǎ búcuò, kējì yīnggāi ràng shēnghuó gèng hǎo, ér búshì ràng rén gèng yuǎn.
Good approach — technology should make life better, not push people apart.

Why HSK 4 Candidates Struggle with Social Phenomena

Discuss life attitudes (marriage, consumption), internet life and its impact, and trending social phenomena.

The biggest challenge for HSK 4 candidates on the social phenomena topic isn't memorizing the 13 core words — it's understanding how they combine in extended contexts. HSK 4 social phenomena questions typically require you to understand cause and effect, opinion shifts, or comparative judgements, not just basic vocabulary recognition.

Key vocabulary to anchor your understanding: 安检 (ānjiǎn, security check), 法 (fǎ, law), 法律 (fǎlǜ, law), 改变 (gǎibiàn, to change). These words frequently appear in HSK 4 listening dialogues (听力) and reading passages (阅读), often with grammar patterns like complex-sentences and rhetorical.

Social Phenomena FAQ / 常见问题

How many HSK 4 words cover social phenomena?

The HSK 4 official syllabus has 13 core words specifically for the social phenomena task scenario. These come from 1 sub-topic categories: social-phenomena. Mastering these 13 words gives you 70-80% comprehension on social phenomena-themed questions in HSK 4.

Which HSK 4 grammar points are most relevant to social phenomena?

complex-sentences and rhetorical appear most frequently in social phenomena contexts. The HSK 4 syllabus expects you to not only understand but also produce these patterns when they involve social phenomena vocabulary.

What's the difference between HSK 3 and HSK 4 expectations on social phenomena?

HSK 3 expects simple statements about social phenomena (e.g., basic facts and short descriptions). HSK 4 raises the bar to "有一定复杂度" — handling extended contexts with opinions, comparisons, and reasoning. You'll need to express feelings (感受) and views (看法) about social phenomena, not just describe them.
