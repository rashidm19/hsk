Official Syllabus Requirement / 大纲要求

能听懂关于职业与工作经历、单位情况的有一定复杂度的问题。如求职、打工、职位变动、招聘应聘、考核面试、工作环境与待遇等。

Scenario Dialogue / 情景对话

A job interview: introducing professional experience. Read it aloud twice: once for meaning, once for fluency — the syllabus tests this task across listening and speaking.

A
请简单介绍一下您的工作经历。
Qǐng jiǎndān jièshào yíxià nín de gōngzuò jīnglì.
Please briefly introduce your work experience.
B
我毕业后在一家广告公司做了三年设计，去年开始负责整个团队。
Wǒ bìyè hòu zài yì jiā guǎnggào gōngsī zuò le sān nián shèjì, qùnián kāishǐ fùzé zhěnggè tuánduì.
After graduating I worked three years as a designer at an ad company, and since last year I have led the whole team.
A
您为什么想换工作呢？
Nín wèishénme xiǎng huàn gōngzuò ne?
Why do you want to change jobs?
B
贵公司的发展方向和我的职业计划更符合，我希望有更大的发展空间。
Guì gōngsī de fāzhǎn fāngxiàng hé wǒ de zhíyè jìhuà gèng fúhé, wǒ xīwàng yǒu gèng dà de fāzhǎn kōngjiān.
Your company's direction fits my career plan better — I am looking for more room to grow.
A
我们提供五险一金，试用期三个月。您最快什么时候能入职？
Wǒmen tígōng wǔ xiǎn yì jīn, shìyòngqī sān gè yuè. Nín zuì kuài shénme shíhou néng rùzhí?
We offer full insurance and benefits, with a three-month probation. How soon could you start?
B
下个月一号就可以。
Xià gè yuè yī hào jiù kěyǐ.
The first of next month.

Why HSK 4 Candidates Struggle with Career & Company

Discuss job seeking, work experiences, career changes, recruitment, interviews, work environment, and salary/benefits.

The biggest challenge for HSK 4 candidates on the career & company topic isn't memorizing the 34 core words — it's understanding how they combine in extended contexts. HSK 4 career & company questions typically require you to understand cause and effect, opinion shifts, or comparative judgements, not just basic vocabulary recognition.

Key vocabulary to anchor your understanding: 打工 (dǎgōng, to work part-time), 读者 (dúzhě, reader), 干活儿 (gànhuór, to work), 歌手 (gēshǒu, singer). These words frequently appear in HSK 4 listening dialogues (听力) and reading passages (阅读), often with grammar patterns like passive and pivotal-sentences.

Career & Company FAQ / 常见问题

How many HSK 4 words cover career & company?

The HSK 4 official syllabus has 34 core words specifically for the career & company task scenario. These come from 2 sub-topic categories: career, company. Mastering these 34 words gives you 70-80% comprehension on career & company-themed questions in HSK 4.

Which HSK 4 grammar points are most relevant to career & company?

passive and pivotal-sentences appear most frequently in career & company contexts. The HSK 4 syllabus expects you to not only understand but also produce these patterns when they involve career & company vocabulary.

What's the difference between HSK 3 and HSK 4 expectations on career & company?

HSK 3 expects simple statements about career & company (e.g., basic facts and short descriptions). HSK 4 raises the bar to "有一定复杂度" — handling extended contexts with opinions, comparisons, and reasoning. You'll need to express feelings (感受) and views (看法) about career & company, not just describe them.
