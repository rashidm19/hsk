Official Syllabus Requirement / 大纲要求

能听懂关于办公事务、工作表现、职场交往情况的有一定复杂度的询问。如工作安排、工作态度能力、同事相处、团建活动等。

Scenario Dialogue / 情景对话

A manager gives feedback on an employee's project. Read it aloud twice: once for meaning, once for fluency — the syllabus tests this task across listening and speaking.

A
小李，这次的项目报告做得很详细，客户非常满意。
Xiǎo Lǐ, zhè cì de xiàngmù bàogào zuò de hěn xiángxì, kèhù fēicháng mǎnyì.
Xiao Li, this project report was very thorough — the client is very satisfied.
B
谢谢经理！不过中间出了一个小错误，耽误了两天，真不好意思。
Xièxie jīnglǐ! Búguò zhōngjiān chū le yí gè xiǎo cuòwù, dānwu le liǎng tiān, zhēn bù hǎoyìsi.
Thank you! Though I made a small mistake midway that delayed us two days — I am sorry about that.
A
发现问题及时改正就好。你平时加班太多，也要注意身体。
Fāxiàn wèntí jíshí gǎizhèng jiù hǎo. Nǐ píngshí jiābān tài duō, yě yào zhùyì shēntǐ.
What matters is you caught and fixed it promptly. You work overtime a lot — take care of your health too.
B
好的。下个季度我想试着负责一个新项目，您看可以吗？
Hǎo de. Xià gè jìdù wǒ xiǎng shìzhe fùzé yí gè xīn xiàngmù, nín kàn kěyǐ ma?
I will. Next quarter I would like to try leading a new project — would that be all right?
A
当然可以，我相信你的能力。
Dāngrán kěyǐ, wǒ xiāngxìn nǐ de nénglì.
Of course — I have confidence in your ability.

Why HSK 4 Candidates Struggle with Work & Performance

Discuss office tasks, work performance, workplace relationships, and team activities in a professional setting.

The biggest challenge for HSK 4 candidates on the work & performance topic isn't memorizing the 26 core words — it's understanding how they combine in extended contexts. HSK 4 work & performance questions typically require you to understand cause and effect, opinion shifts, or comparative judgements, not just basic vocabulary recognition.

Key vocabulary to anchor your understanding: 安排 (ānpái, to arrange), 办公 (bàngōng, to work in an office), 表现 (biǎoxiàn, to perform), 材料 (cáiliào, material). These words frequently appear in HSK 4 listening dialogues (听力) and reading passages (阅读), often with grammar patterns like pivotal-sentences and passive.

Work & Performance FAQ / 常见问题

How many HSK 4 words cover work & performance?

The HSK 4 official syllabus has 26 core words specifically for the work & performance task scenario. These come from 2 sub-topic categories: office, workplace-social. Mastering these 26 words gives you 70-80% comprehension on work & performance-themed questions in HSK 4.

Which HSK 4 grammar points are most relevant to work & performance?

pivotal-sentences and passive appear most frequently in work & performance contexts. The HSK 4 syllabus expects you to not only understand but also produce these patterns when they involve work & performance vocabulary.

What's the difference between HSK 3 and HSK 4 expectations on work & performance?

HSK 3 expects simple statements about work & performance (e.g., basic facts and short descriptions). HSK 4 raises the bar to "有一定复杂度" — handling extended contexts with opinions, comparisons, and reasoning. You'll need to express feelings (感受) and views (看法) about work & performance, not just describe them.
