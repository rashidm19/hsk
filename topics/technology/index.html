Official Syllabus Requirement / 大纲要求

能听懂关于新技术运用、科普知识、科技成果等相关情况的一般性询问。如扫码支付、无人机等新技术、实用科普知识、简单的研究发现等。

Scenario Dialogue / 情景对话

Helping a parent use mobile payment. Read it aloud twice: once for meaning, once for fluency — the syllabus tests this task across listening and speaking.

A
妈，您装个手机支付吧，买东西扫一下二维码就行。
Mā, nín zhuāng ge shǒujī zhīfù ba, mǎi dōngxi sǎo yíxià èrwéimǎ jiù xíng.
Mom, install mobile payment — when shopping you just scan a QR code.
B
安全吗？我担心把钱弄丢了。
Ānquán ma? Wǒ dānxīn bǎ qián nòng diū le.
Is it safe? I am afraid of losing my money.
A
设置好密码就很安全，比带现金方便多了，还能交水电费。
Shèzhì hǎo mìmǎ jiù hěn ānquán, bǐ dài xiànjīn fāngbiàn duō le, hái néng jiāo shuǐdiànfèi.
With a password set, it is very safe — much more convenient than cash, and you can pay utility bills too.
B
真的吗？那打车也能用吗？
Zhēn de ma? Nà dǎchē yě néng yòng ma?
Really? Can I use it for taxis too?
A
能，用手机叫车，到了直接付款。来，我一步一步教您。
Néng, yòng shǒujī jiào chē, dào le zhíjiē fùkuǎn. Lái, wǒ yí bù yí bù jiāo nín.
Yes — call a cab on your phone and pay automatically when you arrive. Here, I will teach you step by step.
B
好，科技发展得真快，我也得跟上时代。
Hǎo, kējì fāzhǎn de zhēn kuài, wǒ yě děi gēnshàng shídài.
OK. Technology develops so fast — I need to keep up with the times.

Why HSK 4 Candidates Struggle with Technology

Discuss new technology applications like mobile payment and drones, practical science knowledge, and simple research findings.

The biggest challenge for HSK 4 candidates on the technology topic isn't memorizing the 24 core words — it's understanding how they combine in extended contexts. HSK 4 technology questions typically require you to understand cause and effect, opinion shifts, or comparative judgements, not just basic vocabulary recognition.

Key vocabulary to anchor your understanding: 短信 (duǎnxìn, text message), 发送 (fāsòng, to send), 互联网 (hùliánwǎng, internet), 回复 (huífù, to reply). These words frequently appear in HSK 4 listening dialogues (听力) and reading passages (阅读), often with grammar patterns like passive and complement.

Technology FAQ / 常见问题

How many HSK 4 words cover technology?

The HSK 4 official syllabus has 24 core words specifically for the technology task scenario. These come from 2 sub-topic categories: tech, science. Mastering these 24 words gives you 70-80% comprehension on technology-themed questions in HSK 4.

Which HSK 4 grammar points are most relevant to technology?

passive and complement appear most frequently in technology contexts. The HSK 4 syllabus expects you to understand these patterns when they involve technology vocabulary.

What's the difference between HSK 3 and HSK 4 expectations on technology?

HSK 3 expects simple statements about technology (e.g., basic facts and short descriptions). HSK 4 raises the bar to "有一定复杂度" — handling extended contexts with opinions, comparisons, and reasoning. You'll need to express feelings (感受) and views (看法) about technology, not just describe them.
