Scenario Dialogue / 情景对话

A student asks the teacher how to prepare for the exam. Read it aloud twice: once for meaning, once for fluency — the syllabus tests this task across listening and speaking.

A
老师，下个月就要考试了，我听力部分总是错得很多，怎么办？
Lǎoshī, xià gè yuè jiù yào kǎoshì le, wǒ tīnglì bùfen zǒngshì cuò de hěn duō, zěnme bàn?
Teacher, the exam is next month and I keep making mistakes in the listening section. What should I do?
B
听力要靠平时积累。建议你每天听半个小时的录音，先听大意，再注意细节。
Tīnglì yào kào píngshí jīlěi. Jiànyì nǐ měitiān tīng bàn gè xiǎoshí de lùyīn, xiān tīng dàyì, zài zhùyì xìjié.
Listening depends on daily accumulation. Listen to recordings for half an hour every day — first for the main idea, then for details.
A
生词太多影响理解，要不要先背单词？
Shēngcí tài duō yǐngxiǎng lǐjiě, yào bu yào xiān bèi dāncí?
Too many new words affect my understanding — should I memorize vocabulary first?
B
背单词和做练习要同时进行，光背不用很快就忘了。
Bèi dāncí hé zuò liànxí yào tóngshí jìnxíng, guāng bèi bú yòng hěn kuài jiù wàng le.
Do both at the same time — if you only memorize without using them, you forget quickly.
A
明白了，我按您说的方法试试。谢谢老师！
Míngbai le, wǒ àn nín shuō de fāngfǎ shìshi. Xièxie lǎoshī!
Got it — I will try the method you suggested. Thank you!

Why HSK 4 Candidates Struggle with Education & Learning

The biggest challenge for HSK 4 candidates on the education & learning topic isn't memorizing the 38 core words — it's understanding how they combine in extended contexts. HSK 4 education & learning questions typically require you to understand cause and effect, opinion shifts, or comparative judgements, not just basic vocabulary recognition.

Key vocabulary to anchor your understanding: 报考 (bàokǎo, to register for an exam), 背 (bēi, to carry on back), 本科 (běnkē, undergraduate degree), 笔试 (bǐshì, written exam). Education & Learning FAQ / 常见问题

How many HSK 4 words cover education & learning?

The HSK 4 official syllabus has 38 core words specifically for the education & learning task scenario. These come from 1 sub-topic categories: study. Mastering these 38 words gives you 70-80% comprehension on education & learning-themed questions in HSK 4.

Which HSK 4 grammar points are most relevant to education & learning?

adverbs and complement appear most frequently in education & learning contexts. The HSK 4 syllabus expects you to not only understand but also produce these patterns when they involve education & learning vocabulary.

What's the difference between HSK 3 and HSK 4 expectations on education & learning?

HSK 3 expects simple statements about education & learning (e.g., basic facts and short descriptions). HSK 4 raises the bar to "有一定复杂度" — handling extended contexts with opinions, comparisons, and reasoning. You'll need to express feelings (感受) and views (看法) about education & learning, not just describe them.
