Official Syllabus Requirement / 大纲要求

能听懂关于休闲活动情况及感受、看法的有一定复杂度的询问。如阅读、网络活动、运动、健身、旅行、聚会等。

Scenario Dialogue / 情景对话

Talking about hobbies and planning a weekend trip. Read it aloud twice: once for meaning, once for fluency — the syllabus tests this task across listening and speaking.

A
周末你一般做什么？
Zhōumò nǐ yìbān zuò shénme?
What do you usually do on weekends?
B
我喜欢爬山和照相，大自然能让人放松。你呢？
Wǒ xǐhuan páshān hé zhàoxiàng, dàzìrán néng ràng rén fàngsōng. Nǐ ne?
I like hiking and photography — nature helps you relax. And you?
A
我对弹钢琴感兴趣，已经坚持练了三年了。
Wǒ duì tán gāngqín gǎn xìngqù, yǐjīng jiānchí liàn le sān nián le.
I am interested in playing piano — I have kept at it for three years now.
B
厉害！对了，这个周末天气不错，一起去郊区爬山怎么样？
Lìhai! Duì le, zhège zhōumò tiānqì búcuò, yìqǐ qù jiāoqū páshān zěnmeyàng?
Impressive! By the way, the weather this weekend looks good — how about hiking in the suburbs together?
A
好主意！正好放松一下，我把照相机也带上。
Hǎo zhǔyi! Zhènghǎo fàngsōng yíxià, wǒ bǎ zhàoxiàngjī yě dài shang.
Great idea! A perfect chance to relax — I will bring my camera too.

Why HSK 4 Candidates Struggle with Hobbies & Leisure

Discuss leisure activities, reading, internet activities, sports, fitness, travel, and parties. Share feelings and opinions about these activities.

The biggest challenge for HSK 4 candidates on the hobbies & leisure topic isn't memorizing the 21 core words — it's understanding how they combine in extended contexts. HSK 4 hobbies & leisure questions typically require you to understand cause and effect, opinion shifts, or comparative judgements, not just basic vocabulary recognition.

Key vocabulary to anchor your understanding: 播放 (bōfàng, to broadcast), 参观 (cānguān, to visit (a place)), 电视剧 (diànshìjù, TV series), 度假 (dùjià, to go on holiday). These words frequently appear in HSK 4 listening dialogues (听力) and reading passages (阅读), often with grammar patterns like adverbs and complex-sentences.

Hobbies & Leisure FAQ / 常见问题

How many HSK 4 words cover hobbies & leisure?

The HSK 4 official syllabus has 21 core words specifically for the hobbies & leisure task scenario. These come from 1 sub-topic categories: leisure. Mastering these 21 words gives you 70-80% comprehension on hobbies & leisure-themed questions in HSK 4.

Which HSK 4 grammar points are most relevant to hobbies & leisure?

adverbs and complex-sentences appear most frequently in hobbies & leisure contexts. The HSK 4 syllabus expects you to not only understand but also produce these patterns when they involve hobbies & leisure vocabulary.

What's the difference between HSK 3 and HSK 4 expectations on hobbies & leisure?

HSK 3 expects simple statements about hobbies & leisure (e.g., basic facts and short descriptions). HSK 4 raises the bar to "有一定复杂度" — handling extended contexts with opinions, comparisons, and reasoning. You'll need to express feelings (感受) and views (看法) about hobbies & leisure, not just describe them.
