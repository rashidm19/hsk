Official Syllabus Requirement / 大纲要求

能听懂关于就医情况、健康生活情况的有一定复杂度的询问。如生病症状、受伤情况、健康观念和常识等。

Scenario Dialogue / 情景对话

Seeing a doctor about a cold. Read it aloud twice: once for meaning, once for fluency — the syllabus tests this task across listening and speaking.

A
大夫，我从昨天开始咳嗽，还有点儿发烧。
Dàifu, wǒ cóng zuótiān kāishǐ késou, hái yǒudiǎnr fāshāo.
Doctor, I have been coughing since yesterday and have a slight fever.
B
我先给你量一下体温。三十八度二，是有点儿发烧。嗓子疼吗？
Wǒ xiān gěi nǐ liáng yíxià tǐwēn. Sānshíbā dù èr, shì yǒudiǎnr fāshāo. Sǎngzi téng ma?
Let me take your temperature first. 38.2 — yes, a slight fever. Does your throat hurt?
A
疼，咽东西的时候特别明显。
Téng, yàn dōngxi de shíhou tèbié míngxiǎn.
Yes, especially when I swallow.
B
是感冒引起的。我给你开点儿药，按时吃，多喝水，注意休息。
Shì gǎnmào yǐnqǐ de. Wǒ gěi nǐ kāi diǎnr yào, ànshí chī, duō hē shuǐ, zhùyì xiūxi.
It is caused by a cold. I will prescribe some medicine — take it on time, drink plenty of water, and rest well.
A
好的。需要打针吗？
Hǎo de. Xūyào dǎzhēn ma?
OK. Do I need an injection?
B
不用，吃三天药应该就好了。要是还发烧，再来医院检查。
Búyòng, chī sān tiān yào yīnggāi jiù hǎo le. Yàoshi hái fāshāo, zài lái yīyuàn jiǎnchá.
No need — three days of medicine should do it. If the fever continues, come back for a check-up.

Why HSK 4 Candidates Struggle with Health & Medical

Discuss symptoms, medical visits, health conditions, and healthy lifestyle concepts. At HSK 4 you need to describe illness experiences in detail and discuss health opinions.

The biggest challenge for HSK 4 candidates on the health & medical topic isn't memorizing the 25 core words — it's understanding how they combine in extended contexts. HSK 4 health & medical questions typically require you to understand cause and effect, opinion shifts, or comparative judgements, not just basic vocabulary recognition.

Key vocabulary to anchor your understanding: 打针 (dǎzhēn, to get an injection), 大夫 (dàifu, doctor), 肚子 (dùzi, stomach), 汗 (hàn, sweat). These words frequently appear in HSK 4 listening dialogues (听力) and reading passages (阅读), often with grammar patterns like ba-sentence and complement.

Health & Medical FAQ / 常见问题

How many HSK 4 words cover health & medical?

The HSK 4 official syllabus has 25 core words specifically for the health & medical task scenario. These come from 1 sub-topic categories: health. Mastering these 25 words gives you 70-80% comprehension on health & medical-themed questions in HSK 4.

Which HSK 4 grammar points are most relevant to health & medical?

ba-sentence and complement appear most frequently in health & medical contexts. The HSK 4 syllabus expects you to not only understand but also produce these patterns when they involve health & medical vocabulary.

What's the difference between HSK 3 and HSK 4 expectations on health & medical?

HSK 3 expects simple statements about health & medical (e.g., basic facts and short descriptions). HSK 4 raises the bar to "有一定复杂度" — handling extended contexts with opinions, comparisons, and reasoning. You'll need to express feelings (感受) and views (看法) about health & medical, not just describe them.
