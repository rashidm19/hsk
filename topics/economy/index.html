Scenario Dialogue / 情景对话

Chatting about live-stream shopping and prices. Read it aloud twice: once for meaning, once for fluency — the syllabus tests this task across listening and speaking.

A
现在直播带货特别火，你买过吗？
Xiànzài zhíbō dàihuò tèbié huǒ, nǐ mǎi guo ma?
Live-stream shopping is really popular now — have you bought anything that way?
B
买过几次，价格确实比平时便宜，但也容易买一堆用不上的东西。
Mǎi guo jǐ cì, jiàgé quèshí bǐ píngshí piányi, dàn yě róngyì mǎi yì duī yòng bu shàng de dōngxi.
A few times. Prices really are lower, but it is easy to end up with a pile of things you never use.
A
是啊，促销的时候大家都冲动消费。
Shì a, cùxiāo de shíhou dàjiā dōu chōngdòng xiāofèi.
Yes — everyone shops impulsively during promotions.
B
我现在列好购物单再买，需要什么买什么，节约不少钱。
Wǒ xiànzài liè hǎo gòuwùdān zài mǎi, xūyào shénme mǎi shénme, jiéyuē bù shǎo qián.
Now I make a shopping list first and only buy what I need — it saves quite a bit.
A
这个习惯好，理性消费才是真省钱。
Zhège xíguàn hǎo, lǐxìng xiāofèi cái shì zhēn shěng qián.
Good habit — rational spending is the real way to save money.

Why HSK 4 Candidates Struggle with Economic Phenomena

Discuss trending products, new business models (online stores, short videos, delivery economy), and economic conditions.

The biggest challenge for HSK 4 candidates on the economic phenomena topic isn't memorizing the 42 core words — it's understanding how they combine in extended contexts. HSK 4 economic phenomena questions typically require you to understand cause and effect, opinion shifts, or comparative judgements, not just basic vocabulary recognition.

Key vocabulary to anchor your understanding: 出口 (chūkǒu, exit), 打折 (dǎzhé, to discount), 低价 (dījià, low price), 费用 (fèiyong, cost). These words frequently appear in HSK 4 listening dialogues (听力) and reading passages (阅读), often with grammar patterns like complex-sentences and adverbs.

Economic Phenomena FAQ / 常见问题

How many HSK 4 words cover economic phenomena?

The HSK 4 official syllabus has 42 core words specifically for the economic phenomena task scenario. These come from 1 sub-topic categories: economy. Mastering these 42 words gives you 70-80% comprehension on economic phenomena-themed questions in HSK 4.

Which HSK 4 grammar points are most relevant to economic phenomena?

complex-sentences and adverbs appear most frequently in economic phenomena contexts. The HSK 4 syllabus expects you to not only understand but also produce these patterns when they involve economic phenomena vocabulary.

What's the difference between HSK 3 and HSK 4 expectations on economic phenomena?

HSK 3 expects simple statements about economic phenomena (e.g., basic facts and short descriptions). HSK 4 raises the bar to "有一定复杂度" — handling extended contexts with opinions, comparisons, and reasoning. You'll need to express feelings (感受) and views (看法) about economic phenomena, not just describe them.
